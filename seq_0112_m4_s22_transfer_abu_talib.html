Abū Ṭālib
The Guardian
The Blood Tie
He was the Full Brother (Shaqīq) of 'Abdullāh. They shared the same mother, creating the strongest possible bond among uncles.
The Character
Though financially poor, he was a Sayyid (Chief) known for his nobility, generosity, and immense social standing in Quraysh.
The Will
'Abd al-Muṭṭalib specifically entrusted the Prophet ﷺ to him on his deathbed, knowing his compassionate nature.
