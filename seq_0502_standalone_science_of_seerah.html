The Science of Seerah

Sources & Methodology of Prophetic Biography

Primary Sources

  • The Noble Qur'an

    The most authentic and primary source, referencing key events (Badr, Uhud, Hunayn) and the Prophet's character.

  • Authentic Hadith (Sunnah)

    Narrations recorded in Sahih al-Bukhari, Muslim, and other canonical collections detailing specific incidents and sayings.

  • Early Biographies (Maghazi/Siyar)

    Specialized works by early scholars like Ibn Ishaq (d. 151 AH), Ibn Hisham, and Al-Waqidi focused on historical chronology.
