Key Takeaways

Module 3 Summary
Divine Selection

The miraculous preservation of 'Abdullāh (through the ransom) demonstrates God's specific plan to ensure the birth of the Prophet ﷺ from a purified lineage.

Divine Protection

The Year of the Elephant proved that the Ka'bah has a Lord who protects it. It established the sanctity of Makkah beyond human capability.

Strategic Context

The event elevated Quraysh's status to "People of God" (*Ahl Allāh*), creating the political immunity and economic stability needed for the Prophet's mission.

Ultimate Tawakkul

'Abd al-Muṭṭalib's response teaches the balance of action and reliance: doing what is within human power, then entrusting the impossible to God.

"History is not random; it is the unfolding of Divine Will."
