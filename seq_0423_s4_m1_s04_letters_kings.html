Letters to Kings

The Global Call to Islam (Muḥarram 7 AH)

The Silver Seal

When told that kings do not read unsealed letters, the Prophet ﷺ ordered a silver ring to be made.

"Muḥammad Rasūl Allāh"

The letters invited the rulers to Islam with a unified message:
"Accept Islam and you will be safe; Allah will grant you a double reward."
