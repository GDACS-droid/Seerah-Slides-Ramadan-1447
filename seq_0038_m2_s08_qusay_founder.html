Quṣayy ibn Kilāb

The Unifier of Quraysh (Al-Mujammi')
قصي بن كلاب
Meaning
"The Distant One"
Origin
Raised in Syria (Levant)
Era
Born ~400 CE

The Return

He returned to Makkah as a young man to reclaim his birthright, finding his people scattered and weak.

The Alliance

He married Ḥubbā, the daughter of the Khuzā'ah chief, positioning himself for leadership through strategic diplomacy.

The Consolidation

He expelled Khuzā'ah and united the Quraysh clans, settling them around the Ka'bah. He was called Al-Mujammi' (The Gatherer).

"He was the first of Banū Ismā'īl to assume full authority over the Sacred House."
