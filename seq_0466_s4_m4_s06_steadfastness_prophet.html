The Steadfastness

The Anchor in the Storm

أَنَا النَّبِيُّ لاَ كَذِبْ ... أَنَا ابْنُ عَبْدِ الْمُطَّلِبْ
"I am the Prophet, no lie!
I am the son of 'Abdul-Muṭṭalib!"
While 12,000 fled, he rode his white mule towards the enemy, challenging them.

The Voice of Al-'Abbās

The Prophet ﷺ ordered his uncle Al-'Abbās (who had a booming voice) to call out:
"O People of the Tree! O People of Surah Al-Baqarah!"

The Rally

Upon hearing the call, the Companions turned back immediately, responding "Labbayk! Labbayk!" (Here we are!), turning the tide of the battle.
