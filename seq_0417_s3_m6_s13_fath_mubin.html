Analytical: The Manifest Victory

"Indeed, We have given you, [O Muḥammad], a clear conquest (Fatḥan Mubīna)..."

Surah Al-Fatḥ (48:1)

Political Recognition

For the first time, Quraysh treated the Muslims not as rebels or runaways, but as a sovereign state with equal status. This legitimized the Islamic State in the eyes of all Arabia.

Explosion of Da'wah

Peace removed the barrier of fear. People mixed freely and heard the Quran. In the 2 years of the truce, more people entered Islam than in the entire previous 18 years combined.

Strategic Focus

By neutralizing the southern threat (Quraysh), the Prophet ﷺ was finally free to deal with the existential threat in the north: the fortress of Khaybar.

"There was no victory in Islam greater than this. War was a barrier between people... but when the truce was laid and war laid down its burdens, no one possessed of reason spoke of Islam but that he entered it."
— Imam Ibn Shihāb al-Zuhrī
