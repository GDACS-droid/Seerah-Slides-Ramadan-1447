The Cause of Mu'tah

A Violation of International Law

The Murder of an Envoy

The Prophet ﷺ sent Al-Ḥārith ibn 'Umayr al-Azdī with a letter to the Governor of Buṣrā.
He was intercepted by Shuraḥbīl ibn 'Amr al-Ghassānī (a Roman client), who asked, "Are you a messenger of Muhammad?" Upon confirmation, he was executed.

Casus Belli

Killing an envoy was a universal declaration of war. To ignore this act would mean the loss of all political prestige and would invite invasion. The Prophet ﷺ mobilized an army of 3,000—the largest to date.
