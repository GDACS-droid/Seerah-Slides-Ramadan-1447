The Du'ā' of 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib

The Supplication at the Door
Before retreating to the mountains, 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib went to the Ka'bah, grasped the metal ring of the door, and recited verses of desperate reliance upon God.
Al-Multazam (The Place of Clinging)
يَا رَبِّ إِنَّ المَرْءَ يَمْنَعُ رَحْلَهُ فَامْنَعْ رِحَالَك
"O Lord, a man protects his own dwelling, so protect Your Dwelling."
لاَ يَغْلِبَنَّ صَلِيبُهُمْ وَمِحَالُهُمْ أَبَدًا مِحَالَك
"Let not their cross and their guile ever overcome Your Power."
"The final act of human effort before Divine intervention."
