The Conversion of Giants

The Tipping Point of Quraysh

Khālid ibn al-Walīd

The Military Genius

Prompted by a letter from his brother: "The Prophet asked about you... A man like you cannot remain ignorant of Islam."

He realized that fighting the Truth was futile and set out for Madīnah.

'Amr ibn al-'Āṣ

The Political Genius

After the Treaty of Ḥudaybiyyah, he realized the Prophet's victory was inevitable. He met Khālid on the road, and they entered Madīnah together.
