Waraqah ibn Nawfal

The Christian Scholar of Makkah
ورقة بن نوفل
  • Paternal cousin of Khadījah bint Khuwaylid.
  • Rejected idolatry and adopted Christianity in the Pre-Islamic era.
  • Literate scholar who wrote scriptures in Hebrew and Arabic.
  • Elderly and blind by the time of the first revelation.
"This is the Nāmūs (Archangel Gabriel) who came down to Mūsā."
Significance: He validated the Prophet's experience not as possession or madness, but as the continuation of the Mosaic prophetic tradition.
"Would that I were young and could live up to the time when your people will turn you out."
