The Nūr Narrative

The Light That Passed to Āminah
The Sign on the Forehead

Traditions state that 'Abdullāh possessed a visible radiance (Nūr) on his forehead, recognized by those versed in scriptural signs.

The Transfer

After his marriage to Āminah was consummated, the light disappeared from him and was said to be transferred to the womb that bore the Prophet ﷺ.

النُّور المُحَمَّدِي
Al-Nūr al-Muḥammadī (The Muhammadan Light)

Academic Context

This narrative appears in the genre of Dalā'il al-Nubuwwah. It is treated in classical Sīrah but often transmitted with weak chains; presented more as theological tradition than established history.

"A theological concept emphasizing the purity and selection of the lineage."
