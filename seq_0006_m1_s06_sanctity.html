The Principle of Sanctity (Ḥurmah)

Al-Ḥaram (الحرم): The Concept of Sacred Space
Al-Ḥaram / الحرم

The word "Ḥaram" shares its root with "ḥarām" (forbidden). Makkah is designated as Al-Ḥaram because acts that are permissible elsewhere become strictly forbidden within its boundaries.

  • Prohibition of hunting animals
  • Prohibition of uprooting plants or cutting trees
  • Prohibition of initiating hostilities or warfare
"Whoever enters it shall be safe."
Qur'ān 3:97
"A man would see the murderer of his father performing ṭawāf and would not touch a hair on his head."
— Ibn 'Abbās (RA)
