The Ban on Listening
Unable to match the Qur'an's eloquence, Quraysh resorted to a campaign of noise. They commanded their people not to listen and to cause disruptions whenever it was recited.
"And those who disbelieve say, 'Do not listen to this Qur'an and speak noisily during [the recitation of] it that perhaps you will overcome.'"
— Surah Fuṣṣilat (41:26)
