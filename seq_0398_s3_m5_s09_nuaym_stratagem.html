"War is Deception"

Al-Ḥarb Khad'ah

Nu'aym ibn Mas'ūd
A respected leader of Ghaṭafān, trusted by both Quraysh and the Jews. He secretly accepted Islam and asked the Prophet ﷺ how he could help. The Prophet ﷺ replied: "You are only one man among us, so go and sow discord among them if you can."
To Banū Qurayẓah

"Quraysh and Ghaṭafān are not like you; they can return home if defeated. You live here. If they leave, Muhammad will destroy you. Do not fight alongside them until you take hostages from their nobles as security."

To Quraysh & Ghaṭafān

"The Jews regret betraying Muhammad. To win back his favor, they have plotted to ask you for hostages, whom they will then hand over to Muhammad to be executed. Do not give them a single man."

The Result: When Quraysh asked the Jews to attack, the Jews demanded hostages. Quraysh said, "Nu'aym was right!" Trust evaporated, and the coalition fractured from within.
