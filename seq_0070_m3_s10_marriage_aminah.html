The Marriage of 'Abdullāh and Āminah

The Union of Two Noble Lines
'Abdullāh
Banū Hāshim

The son of the Chief of Makkah ('Abd al-Muṭṭalib). The "Ransomed One" with the light of nobility on his brow.

Āminah
Banū Zuhrah

The daughter of Wahb, the Chief of Banū Zuhrah. Considered the most excellent woman in Quraysh in lineage and status.

بَنُو زُهْرَة
Banū Zuhrah (The Maternal Clan)
"Sa'd ibn Abī Waqqāṣ was later known as the Prophet's maternal uncle from this clan."
