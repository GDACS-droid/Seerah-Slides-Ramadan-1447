The Journey to Yathrib

A Pilgrimage of Memory (Age ~6)

The Travelers

Āminah, the young Muḥammad ﷺ, and Umm Ayman (Barakah).

The Destination

Yathrib (يَثْرِب) — later known as Madīnah.

The Duration

They stayed for approximately one month.

  • Visiting the Grave
    The primary purpose was to visit the grave of his father, 'Abdullāh, who had died there before his birth.
  • Meeting Maternal Kin
    Connecting with Banū al-Najjār, his maternal uncles (akhwāl).
  • Childhood Memories
    He later recalled: "I learned to swim in the well of Banū 'Adī ibn al-Najjār."
Foreshadowing
"This was his first introduction to the city that would, 50 years later, become his permanent home and sanctuary."
