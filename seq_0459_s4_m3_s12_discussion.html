Reflections on the Conquest

1

Power & Mercy

How does the General Amnesty (Al-Ṭulaqā') redefine the concept of victory? Compare this to historical conquests where revenge was the norm. What does this teach about leadership?

2

Sanctity of Makkah

The Prophet ﷺ stated that fighting in Makkah was made lawful for him for only "an hour of a day" and then prohibited forever. What is the significance of this exception and the subsequent restoration of sanctity?

3

Hearts & Minds

How did the "soft conquest" strategy (10,000 fires, amnesty, honoring Abū Sufyān) facilitate the mass conversion of Quraysh compared to a purely military defeat?
