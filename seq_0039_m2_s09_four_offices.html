Quṣayy's Political Genius

The Four Offices (Al-Manāṣib)
الحجابة
Al-Ḥijābah

Custodianship of the Ka'bah. Holding the keys to the Sacred House; no one could enter without his permission.

السقاية
Al-Siqāyah

Providing Water. Collecting and sweetening water for pilgrims (crucial in the desert heat before Zamzam's rediscovery).

الرفادة
Al-Rifādah

Feeding the Pilgrims. A tax levied on Quraysh to provide food for Hajj pilgrims, establishing Makkah's reputation for generosity.

دار الندوة
Dār al-Nadwah

The Assembly. The parliament where war, peace, and marriage contracts were decided.

"He united religious, military, and political authority in one hand—an unprecedented centralization."
