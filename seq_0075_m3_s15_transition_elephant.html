Transition: The Year of the Elephant

The Chronological Anchor
c. 570 CE
عَامُ الفِيل
'Ām al-Fīl

The most famous date marker in pre-Islamic Arabia. Arabs did not use numbers for years; they used major events.

Immortalized in Sūrah al-Fīl (Chapter 105), serving as the backdrop for the Prophet's birth.

"The stage shifts from the private household to the public sanctuary."
