The Ambush at Ḥunayn

"The Earth Was Narrowed For You"

The Setup

It was dawn (Ghalas). The Muslim army was moving through the steep, narrow valley of Ḥunayn. Visibility was low, and confidence was high.

The Trap

Hawāzin archers had occupied every high point, pass, and crevice of the valley, waiting in complete silence.

"Arrows rained down like locusts"

Panic & Flight

The vanguard (Khālid's column, mostly new Makkan converts) broke under the sudden assault. They turned and fled, trampling the main body of the army behind them. The formation collapsed.

"...and [remember] the day of Ḥunayn, when your great numbers pleased you, but it did not avail you at all, and the earth was narrowed for you with its vastness, then you turned back, fleeing."

Surah At-Tawbah (9:25)
