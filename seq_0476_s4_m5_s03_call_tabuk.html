The Roman Threat

News arrived that Heraclius, the Byzantine Emperor, was mobilizing a massive army on the northern borders to crush the rising power of Islam.

Unlike other battles where the destination was kept secret, the Prophet ﷺ announced Tabūk clearly because of the extreme distance and difficulty.
