The Baḥīrā Incident: The Monk

The Watcher on the Roman Frontier
Baḥīrā
Christian Monk
Buṣrā, Syria
A learned scholar of the scriptures who lived in a monastery on the trade route, watching for the signs of the final Prophet.
The Shading Cloud
He observed a solitary cloud that moved with the caravan, specifically shading the young boy from the intense desert sun.
The Bending Tree
When the boy sat under a tree, its branches lowered to provide shade, a sign of nature's submission to a higher authority.
The Unprecedented Feast
Breaking his usual seclusion, he invited the entire caravan to a meal, solely to identify the one who caused these signs.
الرَّاهِب Al-Rāhib (The Monk)
