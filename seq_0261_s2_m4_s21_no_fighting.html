The Command to Restrain

Kaff al-Aydī (Withholding Hands)
Despite the severe persecution, the Prophet ﷺ strictly forbade any physical retaliation. When 'Abd al-Raḥmān ibn 'Awf and others urged fighting, he replied:
"I have been commanded to forgive, so do not fight."
This policy of non-violence was not pacifism, but a strategic necessity to prevent civil war and preserve the moral high ground.
