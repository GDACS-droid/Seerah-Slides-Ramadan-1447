The Urbanization of Makkah

From Scattered Nomads to City Dwellers

The Act of Tabwī' (Settling)

Before Quṣayy, Quraysh lived in the mountains, fearing to build near the Ka'bah. Quṣayy cut down the wild Salam trees and commanded them to build permanent houses in the valley.

The Khiṭaṭ (Quarters)

He divided the land into specific zones (خِطَط) for each clan. This was the first systematic urban planning of Makkah.
