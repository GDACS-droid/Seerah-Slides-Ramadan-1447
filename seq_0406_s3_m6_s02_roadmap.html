Module Roadmap

1

The Vision

The Prophet's dream of entering the Sacred Mosque in peace, heads shaved, and without fear.

2

The Journey

1,400 unarmed pilgrims in Iḥrām marching towards Makkah, signaling purely peaceful intent.

3

The Standoff

Quraysh's panic, Khālid's cavalry interception, and the camel Qaṣwā' refusing to advance.

4

The Treaty

Intense negotiations, the Pledge of Riḍwān, and the controversial terms of the truce.

5

The Victory

"Verily We have granted you a manifest victory." The strategic triumph of peace over war.
