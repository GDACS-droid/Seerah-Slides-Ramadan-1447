Eliminating Threats

External: Bedouin Tribes
Banū Sulaym & Ghaṭafān

Intelligence reported these tribes gathering at Al-Kudr to attack Madīnah, sensing vulnerability or opportunity.

The Response
Preemptive Expeditions: The Prophet ﷺ marched out to meet them. The enemy fled into the desert, and Madīnah's power projection secured the trade routes.
Internal: Subversion
Ka'b ibn al-Ashraf

A wealthy poet and chieftain who traveled to Makkah to incite Quraysh for revenge and composed erotic poetry insulting Muslim women.

The Response
Targeted Operation: The Prophet ﷺ asked, "Who will deal with Ka'b?" Muḥammad ibn Maslamah volunteered and successfully neutralized him, silencing the propaganda.
Strategic Shift: Moving from passive endurance to active deterrence against all threats.
