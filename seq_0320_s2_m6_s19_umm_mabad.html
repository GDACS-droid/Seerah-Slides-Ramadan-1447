The Tent of Umm Ma'bad

The Blessed Guest

The Miracle of Milk

The travelers stopped at her tent seeking provisions. She had nothing but a weak, emaciated sheep that had dried up. The Prophet ﷺ asked permission to milk it. He touched its udder, mentioned Allah's name, and milk flowed abundantly until every vessel was full.

The Description (Shamā'il)

"I saw a man of evident splendor... His face was radiant... In his eyes was a deep blackness... His neck was shining... When he was silent, dignity covered him, and when he spoke, he was eminent and graceful... He was like a branch between two branches, the most beautiful of the three to look at."
— Umm Ma'bad describing the Prophet ﷺ to her husband
