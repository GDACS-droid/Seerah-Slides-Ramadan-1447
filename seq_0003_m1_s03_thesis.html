The Thesis Statement

Arabia Possessed Fiṭrah-Based Monotheism That Underwent Systematic Corruption

The Arabian Peninsula was not a theological vacuum before Islam. It inherited a structured monotheistic tradition from Ibrāhīm and Ismā'īl (عليهما السلام) — a tradition that was gradually dismantled over centuries until the Jāhiliyyah (جاهلية) period required a prophetic restoration.
