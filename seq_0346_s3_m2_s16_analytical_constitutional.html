Constitutional Innovations

  • 1

    The Concept of Ummah

    Political identity based on ideology (faith) rather than biology (lineage).

  • 2

    Pluralism & Citizenship

    Incorporating non-Muslims as full political citizens with rights and duties.

  • 3

    Rule of Law

    Supremacy of a written document over tribal custom and arbitrary chieftainship.

  • 4

    Territorial Sovereignty

    Defining the borders of Madīnah as a sanctuary (Ḥaram), creating a state territory.
