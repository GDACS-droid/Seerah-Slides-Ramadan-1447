Who is Quraysh?

Fihr ibn Mālik is the progenitor of the tribe. Anyone descended from Fihr is a Qurayshī; anyone before him is from Kinānah or Muḍar.

Etymology of "Quraysh"

تقرش Taqarrush "To gather together" (from scattered clans)
قرش Qirsh "Shark" (Dominance: it eats but is not eaten)
"From Fihr onward, the tribe is universally known as Quraysh."
