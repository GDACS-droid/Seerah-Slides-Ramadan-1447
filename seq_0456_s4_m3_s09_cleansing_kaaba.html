Truth Has Arrived

Restoring the House of Allah

جَاءَ الْحَقُّ وَزَهَقَ الْبَاطِلُ ۚ إِنَّ الْبَاطِلَ كَانَ zَهُوقًا
"Truth has come, and falsehood has vanished. Indeed is falsehood, [by nature], ever bound to vanish."
(Surah Al-Isrā': 81)
360 Idols
Surrounding the Kaaba. The Prophet ﷺ pointed at them with a stick, and they fell onto their faces without being touched.
Internal Cleansing
He entered the Kaaba and ordered the erasure of frescoes depicting Prophets Ibrāhīm and Ismā'īl holding divining arrows.
