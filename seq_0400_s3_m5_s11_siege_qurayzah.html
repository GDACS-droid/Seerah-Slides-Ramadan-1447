Siege of Banū Qurayẓah

"Let no one pray 'Asr except at Banū Qurayẓah."

Immediate Mobilization upon Jibrīl's arrival

The 25-Day Siege

The Prophet ﷺ besieged their fortresses for 25 nights. Allāh cast terror (Ru'b) into their hearts, and their morale collapsed as they realized no help was coming from Quraysh.

Unconditional Surrender

They refused the Prophet's judgment initially but eventually agreed to surrender and accept the arbitration of Sa'd ibn Mu'ādh, their former ally from the tribe of Aws.

Divine Justice
The betrayal that nearly destroyed Madīnah was now met with swift and decisive accountability.
