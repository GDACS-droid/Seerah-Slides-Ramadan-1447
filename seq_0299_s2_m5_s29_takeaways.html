Key Takeaways

Module 5 Summary
Global Vision: Islam was never meant to be restricted to Makkah. The Abyssinian migration was the first step in internationalizing the message.
Justice is Universal: The Prophet ﷺ praised a Christian king for his justice, establishing that justice is a value respected regardless of faith.
Quality over Quantity: The conversion of two key individuals (Ḥamzah and 'Umar) changed the political landscape more than hundreds of weaker converts.
Steadfastness: The early Muslims endured torture, exile, and boycott, yet their faith only increased.
