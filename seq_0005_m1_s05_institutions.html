The Ibrahimic Institutions That Survived

Four Practices Ibrāhīm Instituted That Persisted Until the Prophet ﷺ
Practice
Arabic Term
Description
Sanctity of Makkah
الحرمAl-Ḥaram
Prohibition of hunting, harming vegetation, or initiating violence within the sacred boundaries.
Reverence for the Ka'bah
تعظيم الكعبةTa'ẓīm al-Ka'bah
Veneration of the physical structure as the Qiblah and center of worship established by Ibrāhīm.
The Sacred Months
الأشهر الحرمAl-Ashhur al-Ḥurum
Dhū al-Qa'dah, Dhū al-Ḥijjah, Muḥarram, and Rajab. A universally observed cessation of all warfare.
Ḥajj Rites
مناسك الحجManāsik al-Ḥajj
Rituals including Ṭawāf, Sa'ī, animal sacrifice, and Iḥrām, though often mixed with later pagan innovations.
