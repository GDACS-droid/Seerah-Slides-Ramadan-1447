The Four Ḥunafā' of Makkah

The Secret Meeting During the Festival of Idols
"Our people are upon nothing. They worship stones that do not hear, see, or harm. Let us seek the true religion of Ibrāhīm."
Waraqah ibn Nawfal
ورقة بن نوفل
Path Chosen: Christianity

Became a scholar of the scriptures (Gospel/Torah) and awaited the Final Prophet.

'Ubaydullāh ibn Jaḥsh
عبيد الله بن جحش
Path Chosen: Christianity

Accepted Islam initially but later converted to Christianity in Abyssinia.

'Uthmān ibn al-Ḥuwayrith
عثمان بن الحويرث
Path Chosen: Christianity

Traveled to the Byzantine court seeking power and religion.

Zayd ibn 'Amr
زيد بن عمرو
Path Chosen: Pure Monotheism

Rejected both Judaism and Christianity. Searched independently for the Ḥanīfiyyah of Ibrāhīm.
