Expedition of Dhāt al-Salāsil

Strategic Wisdom & Legal Flexibility

The Commander's Decisions

No Fires in Freezing Cold

'Amr ibn al-'Āṣ forbade the army from lighting fires despite the extreme cold.
Reason: To conceal their small numbers from the enemy.

Tayammum over Ghusl

He led the prayer in a state of major impurity after performing dry ablution (Tayammum).
Reason: He feared freezing to death if he bathed in cold water.

No Pursuit

He forbade pursuing the fleeing enemy.
Reason: To prevent being ambushed in unfamiliar territory.

Prophetic Validation

"I remembered the saying of Allah: 'And do not kill yourselves. Surely, Allah is Most Merciful to you.'"

— 'Amr's explanation to the Prophet ﷺ
The Prophet ﷺ laughed and did not rebuke him, validating his Ijtihād and establishing the principle of prioritizing life preservation in extreme conditions.
