Quraysh's Panic

"By Allāh, he shall never enter it upon us by force."

The Vow of Quraysh

The Interception

Quraysh dispatched their best general, Khālid ibn al-Walīd, with 200 elite horsemen to Ghamīm to block the main road to Makkah. Their intent was to provoke a battle or force a retreat.

The Evasion

Upon hearing of Khālid's position, the Prophet ﷺ said: "Who can lead us by a path other than the one they are on?"

He chose a rugged, difficult detour through the rocky terrain to bypass the cavalry and avoid bloodshed.

Strategic Success: The Muslims successfully bypassed the interception and descended into the plain of Ḥudaybiyyah, catching Quraysh off guard.
