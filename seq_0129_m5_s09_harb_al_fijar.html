Ḥarb al-Fijār: The Sacrilegious Wars

Tribal Warfare in the Sacred Months
Why "Fijār" (Sacrilegious)?
The war was named "Fijār" because the tribes violated the sanctity of the Sacred Months (Al-Ashhur al-Ḥurum), during which all fighting was strictly prohibited by ancient Arabian custom.

The Prophet's Role
  • Age: Approximately 14-15 years old.
  • Participation: He did not fight directly or kill anyone. His role was limited to collecting arrows for his uncles to reuse.
  • Significance: He witnessed the futility of tribal violence without becoming an aggressor himself.
