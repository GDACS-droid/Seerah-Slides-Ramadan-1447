The Lion Awakens

The Conversion of Ḥamzah ibn 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib
1
Abū Jahl insults and physically assaults the Prophet ﷺ near al-Ṣafā. A servant woman witnesses the event.
2
Ḥamzah returns from hunting. The servant informs him. Enraged by the dishonor to his clan and nephew, he marches to the Ka'bah.
3
He strikes Abū Jahl with his bow, declaring: "Will you insult him when I follow his religion?"
4
What started as tribal anger solidified into genuine faith after he prayed to God to remove doubt from his heart.
