The Foreshadowing of Yathrib

Death in the Future City of Refuge
Yathrib (Madīnah)
The Past (570 CE)

'Abdullāh falls ill and dies here. He is buried in the home of his maternal uncles, Banū al-Najjār. The father rests in foreign soil.

The Future (622 CE)

The Prophet ﷺ migrates to this very city. He stays with Banū al-Najjār. The city that holds his father becomes his sanctuary.

دَارُ الهِجْرَة
Dār al-Hijrah (The Abode of Migration)

"The Prophet's father rests in the city that would later save his son."

A profound geographical link between the beginning and the end of the prophetic mission.
