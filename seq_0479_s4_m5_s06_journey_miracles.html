The Journey & Miracles

Signs on the Road to Tabūk

The Miracle of Rain

The army ran out of water in the scorching heat and complained to the Prophet ﷺ. He raised his hands in Dua.

Before he lowered them, a cloud appeared and rained specifically on the camp until every container was filled, while the surrounding desert remained dry.

The Ruins of Al-Ḥijr

Passing the ruins of Thamūd (Madā'in Ṣāliḥ), the Prophet ﷺ covered his head and hastened his pace.

"Do not enter the dwellings of those who wronged themselves unless you are weeping, lest what befell them befalls you."

He ordered them to spill the water drawn from its wells and feed the dough made with it to the camels.

The Miracle of Knowledge: When the Prophet's camel went missing, a hypocrite mocked: "He tells you about heavens but doesn't know where his camel is." Revelation informed the Prophet ﷺ exactly where it was (trapped by a tree), exposing the hypocrite and confirming his Prophethood.
