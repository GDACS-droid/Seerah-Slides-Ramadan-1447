The Death of 'Abdullāh

The Prophet ﷺ Born an Orphan of Father
The Tragic Loss
  • Died in Yathrib (Madīnah) at approx. age 25.
  • Passed away while Āminah was still pregnant (or shortly after birth).
  • Buried among his maternal uncles, Banū al-Najjār.
The Modest Inheritance
  • Five camels.
  • A small flock of sheep.
  • A servant girl: Umm Ayman (Barakah).
Yatīm al-Ab (Orphan of Father)
أَلَمْ يَجِدْكَ يَتِيمًا فَآوَىٰ
"Did He not find you an orphan and give shelter?"
Sūrah al-Ḍuḥā (93:6)
"The Prophet ﷺ never knew his father—this is foundational to his identity."
