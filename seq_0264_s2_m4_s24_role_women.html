Women in the Line of Fire

Shared Sacrifice
Direct Targets
Women like Sumayyah, Zinnīrah, and Umm 'Ubays were not spared. They faced the same brutal torture as men, proving their equal status in faith and sacrifice.
Pillars of Support
Figures like Fāṭimah bint al-Khaṭṭāb (sister of 'Umar) and Umm al-Faḍl played crucial roles in hiding scrolls, teaching faith, and supporting the community.
"The first martyr in Islam was a woman (Sumayyah), cementing the foundational role of women in the survival of the early community."
