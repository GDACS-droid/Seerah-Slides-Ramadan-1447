The Lottery of Arrows

Al-Istikhārah bi al-Azlām

The Sanctuary

The ritual took place inside the Ka'bah, before the idol Hubal. This was the highest court of appeal for the Arabs.

الأَزْلَام
Al-Azlām (Divination Arrows)
1
Ten arrows were prepared, each inscribed with the name of one son.
2
The arrows were given to the Sādin (custodian) of the Ka'bah.
3
He shuffled them in the quiver and drew one single arrow.
