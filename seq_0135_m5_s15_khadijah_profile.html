Trade Mission for Khadījah: Context

Khadījah bint Khuwaylid: A Profile
Status & Lineage
Clan: Banū Asad (Quraysh)
Status: Wealthy, noble widow
Age: Approx. 40 (some sources say 28)
Business Model
She was a successful merchant who did not travel herself but hired men to trade on her behalf using a profit-sharing model (Muḍārabah).
الطَّاهِرَة
Al-Ṭāhirah (The Pure One)
The Search for Integrity
She had heard of Muḥammad's ﷺ exceptional reputation for honesty and trustworthiness in a market filled with fraud. She specifically sought him out to lead her caravan to Syria, offering him double the usual commission.
