The Council of War (Shūrā)

The Dilemma

The Pledge of 'Aqabah only obligated the Anṣār to defend the Prophet ﷺ inside Madīnah. Now, facing an offensive war outside the city, the Prophet ﷺ needed their explicit consent. He repeatedly asked: "Advise me, O people!"

"By Allāh, we will not say to you as the Children of Israel said to Moses: 'Go you and your Lord and fight and we will stay here.' Rather, we say: 'Go you and your Lord and fight, and we will fight along with you.'"

Sa'd ibn Mu'ādh
Leader of the Anṣār (Aws)
