The Theological Contradiction

Attributing to Allah What They Detested for Themselves

The Belief

Banāt Allah (بنات الله)

They claimed that the Angels (Malā'ikah) were the daughters of Allah, assigning female gender to the Divine realm.

VS

The Social Reality

Wa'd al-Banāt (وأد البنات)

Yet, they viewed the birth of daughters as a source of shame and practiced female infanticide to avoid social stigma.

"Is it for you the male and for Him the female? That, then, is an unjust division."
(Qur'ān 53:21-22)
