'Abdullāh: The Chosen One

The Most Beloved Son
The Jewel of Quraysh
  • The youngest of the ten sons.
  • The most beloved to his father.
  • Considered the most handsome and noble youth in Makkah.
The Resolve

'Abd al-Muṭṭalib took 'Abdullāh by the hand and led him toward the idols Isāf and Nā'ilah to perform the sacrifice.

The Outcry

The women of Quraysh wept, and his sisters clung to him, begging their father to find another way.
