The Chaos of Improvised Worship

The Absence of Standardized Rituals

The "Better Rock" Logic

"If we found a stone more beautiful than the one we had, we threw the old one away and took the new one."

Total Lack of Sanctity

The "Pile of Dirt" Ritual

"If we could not find a stone, we gathered a pile of dirt, milked a sheep over it to make it firm, and then performed Ṭawāf around it."

Absurdity of Ritual

Religion had devolved into a trivial, material habit devoid of spiritual substance or unified creed (Aqīdah).

Source: Narration of Abū Rajā' al-'Uṭāridī (Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī)
