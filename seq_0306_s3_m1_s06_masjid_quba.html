Masjid Qubā': The First Mosque

مسجد قباء

"A mosque founded on righteousness from the first day is more worthy for you to stand in. Within it are men who love to purify themselves; and Allah loves those who purify themselves."

(Quran 9:108)

Built on the drying floor of Kulthūm ibn Hidm, in the territory of Banū 'Amr ibn 'Awf.

The Prophet ﷺ personally participated in carrying stones, setting the precedent of servant-leadership.

Originally oriented towards Jerusalem (Bayt al-Maqdis).

Established the priority of spiritual institutions over political or military ones.
