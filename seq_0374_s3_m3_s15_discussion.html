Discussion & Reflection

Leadership & Consultation

The Prophet ﷺ accepted strategic advice from a companion regarding the wells, changing his own plan. What does this teach us about the relationship between divine authority and human expertise in leadership?

Tawakkul vs. Strategy

The Muslims utilized scouts, armor, and formations (Asbāb) yet prayed desperately for victory (Tawakkul). How do we practically balance "tying the camel" and "trusting Allāh" in our own challenges?

The Criterion (Al-Furqān)
