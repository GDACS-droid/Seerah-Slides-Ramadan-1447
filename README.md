# Complete Seerah Curriculum Master Deck

This package contains the consolidated slides for the entire Seerah curriculum, covering Sessions I through IV.

## Structure

The slides are organized sequentially by session and module. The filenames are numbered (e.g., `seq_0001_...`) to ensure they appear in the correct chronological order when viewed or presented.

### Session I: The Meccan Period (Part 1)
*   **Module 1:** Al-Usus al-Ibrahimiyyah (The Ibrahimic Foundations)
*   **Module 2:** Nasab Quraysh wa Siyadat Makkah (Lineage of Quraysh & Sovereignty)
*   **Module 3:** The Birth & Upbringing of the Prophet ﷺ
*   **Module 4:** The Revelation (Wahuy) & The Early Call
*   **Module 5:** Open Preaching & The Reaction of Quraysh

### Session II: The Meccan Period (Part 2) & The Hijrah
*   **Module 1:** Persecution & The Migration to Abyssinia
*   **Module 2:** The Year of Sorrow & The Journey to Ta'if
*   **Module 3:** Al-Isra' wal-Mi'raj & The Pledges of Aqabah
*   **Module 4:** The Hijrah: Planning & Execution
*   **Module 5:** The Foundation of the Madinan State

### Session III: The Madinan Period (Part 1)
*   **Module 1:** The Struggle for Stability & The Battle of Badr (Part 1)
*   **Module 2:** Building Institutions (Binā' al-Mu'assasāt)
*   **Module 3:** The Battle of Badr (Part 2)
*   **Module 4:** Post-Badr & The Battle of Uḥud
*   **Module 5:** The Battle of the Trench & Banū Qurayẓah
*   **Module 6:** The Treaty of Ḥudaybiyyah

### Session IV: The Madinan Period (Part 2) & The Conclusion
*   **Module 1:** Khaybar & The Letters to Kings
*   **Module 2:** The Battle of Mu'tah & 'Umrah al-Qaḍā'
*   **Module 3:** The Conquest of Makkah (Fatḥ Makkah)
*   **Module 4:** The Battles of Ḥunayn & Ṭā'if
*   **Module 5:** The Campaign of Tabūk & The Year of Delegations
*   **Module 6:** The Farewell Hajj & The Passing of the Prophet ﷺ

## Usage
*   Open `slide_state.json` to see the full metadata and order.
*   The HTML files are standalone and can be opened in any modern web browser.
*   For presentation mode, simply open the first file and navigate (note: standard browser navigation applies unless a specific slide viewer is used).

## Design Note
All slides follow the "Elegant Academic Minimalism" aesthetic:
*   **Background:** Cream parchment texture
*   **Accents:** Islamic geometric borders in deep emerald, gold, and dark brown
*   **Typography:** Playfair Display (Headings) and Lato (Body)
