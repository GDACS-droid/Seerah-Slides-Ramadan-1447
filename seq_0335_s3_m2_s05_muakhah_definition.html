The Solution: Mu'ākhāh

"Brotherhood Pact"

A formal, divinely-sanctioned pairing of Muhājirūn and Anṣār into bonds of brotherhood that initially superseded blood relations in inheritance and support.

The Venue

House of Anas ibn Mālik

The Participants

90 Men
(45 Muhājirūn + 45 Anṣār)

Legal Status

Binding contract of inheritance
(Later abrogated by Sūrah al-Aḥzāb)
