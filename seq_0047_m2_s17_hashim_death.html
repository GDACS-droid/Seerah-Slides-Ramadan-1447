Hāshim's Final Journey

Marriage, Legacy, and Death

Yathrib

The Strategic Marriage

He married Salmā bint 'Amr from the noble Banū Najjār clan. A crucial alliance that would later support the Prophet's migration.

Shaybah

The Legacy

A son was born with a white streak of hair (شيبة). He remained in Yathrib with his mother while Hāshim continued his travels.

Gaza

The End

Hāshim fell ill and died in Gaza at the young age of ~25. To this day, it is known as Ghazat Hāshim.

"He left behind a young son in Yathrib who was destined to restore the glory of Makkah."
