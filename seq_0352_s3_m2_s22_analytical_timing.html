Why the Change?

Identity Formation (Tamayuz)

Established the Muslim Ummah as a distinct community with its own spiritual center, separate from Jewish tradition.

The Test (Imtiḥān)

"We did not make the Qiblah... except that We might make evident who follows the Messenger from who turns back on his heels." (2:143)

Abrahamic Roots

Reconnected the faith to Ibrāhīm (AS) and the Ka'bah, bypassing the corruption of later traditions and reclaiming the original monotheism.
