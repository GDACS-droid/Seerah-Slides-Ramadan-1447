Arrival at Qubā'

Date 12 Rabī' al-Awwal, Year 1 AH
(~September 622 CE)
Location A settlement 3 miles south of Madīnah proper, inhabited by Banū 'Amr ibn 'Awf.
Host Kulthūm ibn al-Hidm (elder of the tribe).
Duration Approximately 4 days (Monday to Thursday/Friday).
