The Council of War

The Prophetic Vision

The Prophet ﷺ saw a dream indicating the best course of action:

  • Cows Slaughtered: Interpreted as the loss of his companions.
  • Dented Sword: Interpreted as harm to a member of his family.
  • Secure Armor: Interpreted as the city of Madīnah itself.

Strategy: Stay in Madīnah and fight a defensive urban war.

The Public Debate

The Elders & 'Abdullāh ibn Ubayy: Agreed with the Prophet's defensive plan to stay within the city.

The Youth & Zealots: Especially those who missed Badr, urged the Prophet ﷺ to march out to meet the enemy, fearing that staying inside would be seen as cowardice.

Outcome: The majority favored the offensive strategy.

The Point of No Return

"It is not befitting for a Prophet to put on his armor and then take it off until Allāh judges between him and his enemy."
