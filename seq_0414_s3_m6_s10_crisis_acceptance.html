The Crisis of Acceptance

'Umar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb

"Are we not on the Truth and they on Falsehood? Are not our slain in Paradise and theirs in the Fire?"

'Umar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb

"Why then do we accept humiliation in our religion and return while Allāh has not yet judged between us?"

Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ

"I am the Servant of Allāh and His Messenger. I will not disobey His command, and He will not let me perish."

The Alignment of Al-Ṣiddīq

'Umar later went to Abu Bakr with the exact same questions. Abu Bakr gave the exact same answers, word-for-word, as the Prophet ﷺ, demonstrating his complete spiritual alignment.

'Umar later said: "I continued to fast, give charity, pray, and free slaves out of fear for what I had said that day."
