Prophet vs. Messenger

Defining the Status
نَبِيّ
Nabi
One who receives revelation but is not necessarily commanded to convey a new law to a disbelieving people.
Achieved at Iqra (Sūrah al-'Alaq)
رَسُول
Rasul
One who is commanded to deliver a message and warn a people who oppose it.
Achieved at Qum fa-andhir (Sūrah al-Muddaththir)
"He became a Prophet with 'Iqra', and a Messenger with 'al-Muddaththir'."
