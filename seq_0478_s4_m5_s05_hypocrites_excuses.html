The Hypocrites

The Exposure of Falsehood

The Excuse
لَا تَنفِرُوا فِي الْحَرِّ
"Do not march in the heat."

They preferred the shade of their gardens over the struggle in the path of Allah.

The Reality
قُلْ نَارُ جَهَنَّمَ أَشَدُّ حَرًّا
"Say: The fire of Hell is more intense in heat."

(Surah At-Tawbah 9:81)

Surah At-Tawbah is known as Al-Fāḍiḥah (The Exposer) because it revealed the secrets of the Hypocrites.
