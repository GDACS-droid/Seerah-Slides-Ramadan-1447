Analytical Reflection

The Necessity of Secrecy
Was it Fear or Strategy?
The secrecy was not born of cowardice, but of strategic necessity. It was a Sunnah (methodology) for all movements seeking radical change.
Preserving the Seed
A seed must remain buried underground to germinate. If exposed to the sun too early, it burns and dies.
Avoiding Premature Conflict
Early confrontation would have united Quraysh against a tiny, defenseless group before they had the spiritual strength to endure.
