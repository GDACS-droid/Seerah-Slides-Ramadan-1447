Module Objectives

Key Themes & Learning Goals
Trace the Genealogical Line from Ibrāhīm to the Prophet ﷺ.
Analyze Quṣayy ibn Kilāb's consolidation of Meccan power.
Examine the Four Offices (Manāṣib) of Quraysh leadership.
Understand Hāshim's economic innovation (The Īlāf System).
Study 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib and the rediscovery of Zamzam.
