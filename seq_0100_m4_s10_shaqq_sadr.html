Shaqq al-Ṣadr: The Splitting of the Chest

شَقُّ الصَّدْر
The Arrival
Two angels approached him while he played with other children.
The Extraction
They opened his chest and removed a "Black Clot".
The Purification
His heart was washed with Zamzam water in a golden basin.
The Restoration
They returned and sealed his chest.
العَلَقَة السَّوْدَاء Al-'Alaqah al-Sawdā' (The Black Clot)
