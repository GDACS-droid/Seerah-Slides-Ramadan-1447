The First Convert

From the Youth
‘Alī ibn Abī Ṭālib (ra)
Age: ~10 Years Old

Living in the Prophet's household, he witnessed the prayer of the Prophet ﷺ and Khadījah. Though young, he displayed remarkable maturity.

Independent Choice
When invited, he initially wanted to consult his father, Abu Talib. However, after a night of reflection, he decided to accept Islam on his own, reasoning that Allah created him without consulting Abu Talib, so he should worship Him without consulting Abu Talib.
