Phase 2: Chaos & Rumors

The Encirclement

Trapped between Khālid's cavalry from the rear and the returning Meccan infantry, the Muslim ranks shattered. Cohesion was lost.

Friendly Fire

In the dust and confusion, Muslims began striking each other. Ḥusayl ibn Jābir (Al-Yamān) was tragically killed by his own comrades despite shouts to stop.

"I have killed Muhammad!"

Ibn Qami'ah slew Muṣ'ab ibn 'Umayr (who resembled the Prophet ﷺ) and shouted this lie.

The Collapse

Believing the Prophet ﷺ was dead, morale evaporated. Many fled to Madīnah or the mountain; others sat down, dropping their weapons in despair.

"O people! If Muhammad is dead, then the Lord of Muhammad is alive! Rise and die for what he died for!"
Anas ibn Naḍr
