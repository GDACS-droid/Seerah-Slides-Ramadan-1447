Enforced Cessation of Hostilities

These four months constituted a mandatory legal framework where all warfare ceased and tribes observed strict prohibition of violence. Islam retained this institution, confirming its Abrahamic origin rather than abolishing it as a pagan relic.

(Reference: Qur'ān 9:36)
