The Entry Strategy

Four Columns, One Objective

Zubayr ibn al-Awwām

Entered from the North (Kadā'). Planted the Prophet's flag at Al-Ḥajūn and waited for his arrival.

Sa'd ibn 'Ubādah

Entered from the West. The banner was taken from him and given to his son Qays after he shouted "Today is the day of slaughter."

MAKKAH

Abū 'Ubaydah

Entered from the North-West (parallel to the Prophet ﷺ). Entered peacefully with no resistance.

Khālid ibn al-Walīd

The Resistance Sector: Entered from the South (Kudā'). Faced attack from 'Ikrimah and Ṣafwān. Forced to fight back, killing 12-13 polytheists.

Strategic Note: The simultaneous entry from all directions dispersed the Quraysh defense, making organized resistance impossible and minimizing casualties.
