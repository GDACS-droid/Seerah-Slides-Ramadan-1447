Defending the Prophet ﷺ

In the chaos, the Prophet ﷺ was surrounded. His helmet was smashed into his cheek, his tooth was broken, and blood flowed down his face. A small group of "Human Shields" formed a circle of sacrifice around him.

Ṭalḥah ibn 'Ubaydullāh
The Living Martyr

He shielded the Prophet's face with his bare hand against arrows until his fingers were paralyzed. He then carried the Prophet ﷺ on his back up the rock.

"Whoever wants to see a martyr walking on earth, let him look at Ṭalḥah."
Sa'd ibn Abī Waqqāṣ
The Master Archer

The Prophet ﷺ emptied his own quiver for Sa'd, handing him arrows one by one to strike the enemy.

"Shoot, Sa'd! May my father and mother be sacrificed for you!"
Nasībah bint Ka'b
Umm 'Umārah

She abandoned her water-skin and fought with sword and shield. She took 13 severe wounds, including a deep cut to her neck, while deflecting blows from the Prophet.

"Wherever I turned, left or right, I saw her fighting for me."
