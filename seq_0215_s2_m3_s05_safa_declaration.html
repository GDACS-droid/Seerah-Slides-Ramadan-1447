The Mount Ṣafā Declaration

Establishing Credibility Before Warning

01

The Alarm Cry

The Prophet ﷺ climbed Mount Ṣafā and called out "Yā Ṣabāḥāh!" — the traditional Arab warning used only for imminent attacks.

02

The Hypothetical Question

When clans gathered, he asked: "If I told you there was an army behind this mountain about to attack, would you believe me?"

03

The Unanimous Verdict

They replied without hesitation: "Yes, we have never experienced a lie from you." This admission sealed the argument.

يَا صَبَاحَاه

Yā Ṣabāḥāh — The Morning Alarm
