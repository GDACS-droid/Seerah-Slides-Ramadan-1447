The Vulnerability of Having One Son

The Oath of 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib
During the excavation of Zamzam, the clans of Quraysh harassed and bullied him. He had only his eldest son, Al-Ḥārith, to defend him, exposing his lack of tribal manpower ('Izzah).
"O Allah, if You grant me ten sons who grow up to protect me, I will sacrifice one of them for You at the Ka'bah."
