The Divine Command

Transitioning to the Public Phase

فَاصْدَعْ بِمَا تُؤْمَرُ
"Proclaim openly [or cleave the air with] what you are commanded..."
Sūrah Al-Ḥijr 15:94

Linguistic Insight: "Ṣda'" (صَدْع)

The verb ṣada'a literally means "to split" or "to cleave". The command implies the message will rupture the social fabric and shatter the status quo of silence.

Timing
After 3 years of secret Da'wah
Readiness
Critical mass of ~40-50 believers reached
Shift
From Tarbiyah (Formation) to Indhār (Warning)
