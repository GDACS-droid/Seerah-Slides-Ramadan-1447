The Seduction

Ḥuyayy ibn Akhṭab (The Instigator)
"Woe to you, Ka'b! Open the door. I have brought you the glory of the age! I have brought you Quraysh and Ghaṭafān who have pledged not to leave until they uproot Muhammad."
Ka'b ibn Asad (Banū Qurayẓah)
"By God, you have brought me humiliation... Leave me be, for I have seen nothing from Muhammad except truth and loyalty."

Ḥuyayy persisted, shaming Ka'b and finally promising: "If they leave without victory, I will enter your fortress and share your fate." Ka'b yielded.

The Strategic Nightmare

The Shield Breaks

Banū Qurayẓah guarded the southern approach to Madīnah, the only side not protected by the Trench or natural lava fields.

The Vulnerability

The Muslim women and children were sheltered in fortresses near Banū Qurayẓah. With the treaty broken, the families of the defenders were now directly exposed to massacre from the rear.

"The enemy came upon you from above you (The Trench) and from below you (Banū Qurayẓah)..." (Qur'an 33:10)
