Digging the Trench

The Communal Struggle

3,000 men worked in shifts through the freezing winter nights. Hunger was so severe that the Companions tied stones to their stomachs to suppress the pain. The Prophet ﷺ worked alongside them, covered in dust, chanting poetry to lift their spirits.

The Unbreakable Rock

A massive rock obstructed the digging. The Prophet ﷺ struck it three times. Each strike emitted a flash of light, revealing visions of future conquests.

"Allāhu Akbar! I have been given the keys of Syria... the keys of Persia... and the keys of Yemen."
Jābir's Feast

Jābir ibn 'Abdullāh invited the Prophet ﷺ for a small meal (one sheep and some barley). The Prophet ﷺ brought the entire army of 1,000 men.

The food multiplied miraculously; everyone ate until full, and the pot remained brimming with meat and the dough with bread.
