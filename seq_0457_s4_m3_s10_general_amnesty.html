The General Amnesty

Mercy Over Revenge

The Prophet ﷺ asks Quraysh
"O gathering of Quraysh! What do you think I will do to you?"
The People of Makkah Reply
"Good! You are a noble brother, son of a noble brother."
"Go, for you are free."
اذْهَبُوا فَأَنْتُمُ الطُّلَقَاءُ
He quoted Prophet Yūsuf: "No blame will there be upon you today." (12:92)
Al-Ṭulaqā' (The Freed Ones): The term used for the people of Makkah who were forgiven and set free despite their past crimes.
