The Return of Ja'far

A Dual Victory

The Long Separation

After nearly 15 years in Abyssinia, the remaining migrants (including Ja'far ibn Abī Ṭālib and Abū Mūsā al-Ash'arī) arrived in Khaybar just as the victory was secured.

The Resemblance

Ja'far held a special status. The Prophet ﷺ said to him: "You resemble me in your physical creation and your character."

"I do not know which makes me happier: the conquest of Khaybar or the arrival of Ja'far."
— Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ
The Prophet ﷺ stood up to receive him, embraced him warmly, and kissed him on the forehead between the eyes.
