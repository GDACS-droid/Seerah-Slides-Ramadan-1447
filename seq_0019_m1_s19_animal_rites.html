Superstitious Animal Rites

Fabricated Prohibitions Condemned in the Qur'ān
Bahīrah بحيرة

A she-camel with a slit ear whose milk was set aside for idols.

Sā'ibah سائبة

A she-camel let loose for idols and not burdened with loads.

Waṣīlah وصيلة

A she-camel that bore successive females and was spared slaughter.

Ḥām حام

A stallion freed from work after siring many offspring.

"Allah has not appointed [such innovations as] Bahīrah or Sā'ibah or Waṣīlah or Ḥām. But those who disbelieve invent lies against Allah." (Qur'ān 5:103)
