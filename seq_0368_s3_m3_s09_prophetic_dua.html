The Prophetic Plea

Location: The 'Arīsh (Command Post)

"O Allāh, fulfill what You promised me! O Allāh, if this small band of Muslims is destroyed, You will not be worshipped on this earth."

He stretched his hands so high in supplication that his cloak fell from his shoulders, demonstrating his total reliance (Tawakkul) on the Divine.

Abū Bakr al-Ṣiddīq: He embraced the Prophet ﷺ from behind and whispered, "O Messenger of Allāh, this is sufficient for your Lord. He will fulfill what He promised you."
