The Diagnosis: Al-Nāmūs

Linking to the Prophetic Tradition
"This is the Nāmūs who came down to Moses!"
هَذَا النَّامُوسُ الَّذِي نَزَّلَ اللَّهُ عَلَى مُوسَى
Al-Nāmūs (الناموس)

A term used specifically for the Archangel Gabriel (Jibrīl) in the context of bringing divine law. It signifies the bearer of confidential, divine secrets.

Significance: Waraqah instantly connects this experience not to madness or poetry, but to the specific lineage of Musa (Moses) — confirming the Abrahamic continuity.
