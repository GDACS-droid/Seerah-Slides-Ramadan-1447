The Jesus Question

A Dangerous Trap
The Envoys:
"O King, they say terrible things about Jesus, son of Mary. Send for them and ask them what they say about him."
Ja'far:
"We say about him what our Prophet brought to us: He is the servant of God, His Messenger, His Spirit, and His Word which He cast into Mary the blessed Virgin."
This was the most critical moment. If Ja'far had compromised or lied, he would have betrayed his faith. If he had been offensive, he would have been executed. His answer was truthful yet respectful, using shared terminology ("Word," "Spirit") without conceding to the concept of sonship.
