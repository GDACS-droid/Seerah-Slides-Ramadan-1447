Ṣuhayb al-Rūmī (ra)

رَبِحَ البيعُ يا أبا يحيى — The Profitable Transaction
Context: The Hijrah to Madīnah — Ṣuhayb's arrival at Qubā'
When Ṣuhayb attempted hijrah, Quraysh blocked him: "You came to us poor, then you gained wealth among us — now you won't leave with both."
Ṣuhayb responded: "If I give you my wealth, will you let me go?" They agreed — so he gave up all his property and was allowed to migrate.
When he reached the Prophet ﷺ at Qubā', he was told:
"رَبِحَ البيعُ يا أبا يحيى"
"The transaction has profited, O Abū Yaḥyā!"
— Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
وَمِنَ النَّاسِ مَن يَشْرِي نَفْسَهُ ابْتِغَاءَ مَرْضَاتِ اللَّهِ
"And among the people is he who sells himself seeking the pleasure of Allah." — Q 2:207 (widely cited as revealed regarding Ṣuhayb's sacrifice)
