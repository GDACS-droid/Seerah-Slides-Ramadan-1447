Adopting Zayd ibn Ḥārithah: Background

From Noble Birth to Enslavement
Noble Roots
Born free to the noble Arab tribe of Kalb. His father, Ḥārithah, was a man of status, and Zayd was not born into slavery.
The Tragedy
While visiting his mother's tribe, bandits raided the camp. Zayd, then a young boy (approx. 8 years old), was kidnapped.
The Market
Taken to the market of Ukāẓ, he was sold for 400 dirhams to Ḥakīm ibn Ḥizām, a nephew of Khadījah.
The Gift
Ḥakīm brought him to Makkah. Khadījah chose him from among the slaves, and later gifted him to the Prophet ﷺ upon their marriage.
