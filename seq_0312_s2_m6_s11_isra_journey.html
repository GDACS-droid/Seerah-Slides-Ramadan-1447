Al-Isrā': The Night Journey

From Makkah to Jerusalem
Bayt al-Maqdis
(Jerusalem)

Al-Burāq

The Prophet ﷺ was transported on Al-Burāq, a white beast smaller than a mule but larger than a donkey, whose single stride reached the horizon.

The Gathering of Prophets

Upon arriving at Al-Masjid Al-Aqsa, he found all previous Prophets (124,000 in some narrations) gathered, waiting for him.

Imam of the Messengers

Jibrīl (as) motioned for him to lead the prayer. This signified the transfer of spiritual leadership (Imāmah) to him and his Ummah, sealing the legacy of all previous revelations.

The Test of Fitrah

He was offered milk and wine. He chose the milk. Jibrīl said: "You have chosen the Fitrah (natural disposition). Had you chosen the wine, your Ummah would have gone astray."
