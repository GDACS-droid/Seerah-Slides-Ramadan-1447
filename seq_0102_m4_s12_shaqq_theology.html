Theological Interpretation

The Removal
حَظُّ الشَّيْطَان
Removal of the "Portion of Satan" — a protective miracle that removed the Prophet's susceptibility to whisperings.
The Preparation
تَطْهِيرُ الوِعَاء
"Purification of the Vessel." The heart was washed with Zamzam to ready it for Revelation.
أَلَمْ نَشْرَحْ لَكَ صَدْرَك
"Did We not expand for you your chest?"
Sūrah ash-Sharḥ (94:1)
