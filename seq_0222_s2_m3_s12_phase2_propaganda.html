Phase 2: Propaganda

The War of Narratives
Coordinated Distortion
When mockery failed, they escalated to organized misinformation, especially during the Hajj season to warn pilgrims away.
The "Sorcerer" (Sāḥir) Narrative:
They claimed his words had a magical effect that separated a man from his father, brother, and wife.
Strategic Placement:
They positioned men on every road leading to Makkah to intercept travelers and "warn" them about the "dangerous man" inside.
