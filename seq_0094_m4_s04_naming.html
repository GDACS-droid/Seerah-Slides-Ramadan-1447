The Naming: "Muḥammad"

مُحَمَّد
The Praised One

"I wanted him to be praised by God in the heavens and by people on earth."

— 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib
Unprecedented
Virtually unknown name in Arabia at the time.
Prophetic Sign
A form of Irhaṣāt (precursor to prophethood).
Root Meaning
From ḥ-m-d (to praise, to thank).
