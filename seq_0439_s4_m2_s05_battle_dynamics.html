Zayd's Martyrdom

Ja'far al-Ṭayyār (The Flyer)

He took the banner. His right hand was cut off; he held it with his left. His left was cut off; he held it with his chest until he was killed.
"Allah replaced his hands with two wings with which he flies in Paradise."

3

'Abdullāh's Stand

He hesitated for a moment due to the sheer horror, then recited poetry to conquer his fear. He took the banner and fought until he too joined his companions.
