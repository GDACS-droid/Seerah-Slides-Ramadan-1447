The Birth: Date & Context

Rabī' al-Awwal
12th
'Ām al-Fīl (c. 570 CE)
Scholarly Consensus
Widely accepted as Monday, 12th Rabī' al-Awwal. Alternative views suggest 8th, 9th, or 10th.
Location
Born in the house of Abū Ṭālib in the Shi'b of Banū Hāshim.
Status at Birth
Born an orphan (Yatīm) — his father 'Abdullāh had died months prior.
