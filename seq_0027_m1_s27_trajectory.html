The Theological Trajectory

From Monotheism to Corruption and Restoration

Foundation

Ibrāhīm & Ismā'īl
Establishment of pure Tawḥīd and the rites of Hajj.

Corruption

'Amr ibn Luḥay
Introduction of the first idol (Hubal) and alteration of the Talbiyah.

Jāhiliyyah

The Dark Era
360 idols, superstition, and loss of the Abrahamic creed.

Remnants

The Ḥunafā'
Isolated individuals seeking the lost truth amidst the chaos.

Restoration

Muḥammad ﷺ
The final messenger sent to restore the Millah of Ibrāhīm.

Islam did not bring a "new" religion, but restored the original monotheism of the land.
