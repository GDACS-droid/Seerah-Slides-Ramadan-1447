The Corruption of the Talbiyah

From Tawḥīd to Shirk in a Single Liturgical Formula
The Ibrahimic Original
لبيك اللهم لبيك، لبيك لا شريك لك لبيك
"Here I am, O Allah, here I am. Here I am, You have no partner, here I am."
The Innovation of 'Amr ibn Luḥay
لبيك اللهم لبيك، لبيك لا شريك لك إلا شريكاً هو لك، تملكه وما ملك
"Here I am, O Allah... You have no partner except for a partner who belongs to You, and You control him and all he controls."
Theological Implication: Hierarchical Polytheism
This formula preserved Allah as the supreme "Big Boss" deity while introducing subordinate partners, resembling ancient Greek theology rather than pure monotheism.
