The Baḥīrā Incident: Context

Journey to Syria with Abū Ṭālib (Age ~12)
Makkah
The Plea
As Abū Ṭālib prepared the caravan for al-Shām, the young Muḥammad ﷺ clung to his uncle's mount, weeping and begging not to be left behind. Moved by compassion, Abū Ṭālib relented: "By Allah, I will take him with me, and we shall never part."
Buṣrā (Syria)
The Frontier
The caravan stopped at Buṣrā, a major trading hub on the Roman frontier. It was a place of cross-cultural encounter, where Arab merchants met the Byzantine world—and where a certain monk resided.
