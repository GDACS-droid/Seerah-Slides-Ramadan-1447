The General Attack

Iron Discipline

The Muslims maintained a rigid defensive formation, using long-range archery to check the chaotic Meccan charge. They did not break ranks or engage prematurely.

"Do not attack until I order you."

The Counter-Attack

At the critical moment, the Prophet ﷺ threw a handful of dust at the enemy, blinding them, and ordered the general advance. The disciplined line surged forward as one.

"May the faces be disgraced!" (Shāhat al-Wujūh)

Divine Intervention

1,000 Angels descended in ranks, led by Jibrīl. They struck terror into the hearts of the Quraysh and physically aided the believers, targeting the enemy's necks and fingertips.

"I am with you, so strengthen those who have believed." (8:12)
