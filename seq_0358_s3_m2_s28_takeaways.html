Key Takeaways

  • Institutional State Building

    The Prophet ﷺ built Madīnah on solid institutions (Mosque, Market, Brotherhood), not just personal charisma, ensuring sustainability.

  • Identity Formation

    The change of Qiblah and the Adhān gave the Muslims a distinct spiritual identity, separating them from previous traditions.

  • Justice & Pluralism

    The Ṣaḥīfah established the rule of law and rights for minorities, setting a precedent for a pluralistic society based on justice.
