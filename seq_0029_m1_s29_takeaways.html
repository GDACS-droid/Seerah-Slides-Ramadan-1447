Key Takeaways

Summary of Module 1

Monotheistic Roots

Arabia's original faith was the pure Tawḥīd of Ibrāhīm; paganism came later.

The Catalyst of Corruption

'Amr ibn Luḥay altered the Hajj rites and introduced idol worship.

The Spiritual Vacuum

The Jāhiliyyah lacked Scripture and Prophethood, leading to superstition.

Islam as Restoration

The Prophet restored the Millah of Ibrāhīm rather than inventing a new religion.

"The Truth has come, and falsehood has vanished."
