The Dream of Zamzam

Visions in the Ḥijr Ismā'īl

The Setting

'Abd al-Muṭṭalib was sleeping in the Ḥijr (the semi-circular enclosure of the Ka'bah) when a figure appeared to him in a dream for four consecutive nights.
