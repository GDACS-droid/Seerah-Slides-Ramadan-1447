The Great Desertion

Just before the battle, 'Abdullāh ibn Ubayy (leader of the Hypocrites) withdrew with 300 men—one-third of the army—claiming the Prophet ﷺ had ignored his advice to stay in Madīnah.

"He obeyed the young men and disobeyed me. Why should we kill ourselves?"

The Strategic Anchor

"Protect our backs! If you see us being snatched by birds, do not leave this post until I send for you."
