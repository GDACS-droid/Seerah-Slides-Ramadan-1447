The Sermon at Arafat

Khutbat al-Wadā' (The Farewell Sermon)

Sanctity of Life & Property

"Your blood, your property, and your honor are sacred to you, like the sanctity of this day of yours, in this month of yours, in this city of yours."

Abolition of Usury (Riba)

"All usury of the Jahiliyyah is abolished. And the first usury I abolish is that of my uncle Abbas ibn Abd al-Muttalib."

Rights of Women

"Fear Allah concerning women! Verily you have taken them on the security of Allah... They have rights over you and you have rights over them."

Universal Equality

"An Arab has no superiority over a non-Arab, nor a white over a black, except by piety (Taqwa). All of you are from Adam, and Adam is from dust."

"O Allah, bear witness!" The Prophet ﷺ raised his finger to the sky and pointed to the people
