The Death of Abū Ṭālib

The Final Moments

The Plea of the Prophet ﷺ

The Prophet ﷺ: "O Uncle, say 'Lā ilāha illā Allāh', a word with which I may argue for you before Allah."

He stood by his uncle's bedside, desperate to save the man who had protected him for over 40 years, hoping for a single declaration of faith.

The Pressure of Quraysh

Abū Jahl & Abdullah b. Abī Umayyah: "O Abū Ṭālib! Will you leave the religion of Abdul Muṭṭalib?"

They kept repeating this, appealing to his tribal pride and ancestry, until his last words were confirmed: "On the religion of Abdul Muṭṭalib."

The Result: He died without professing Islam. The Prophet ﷺ said: "I will keep asking forgiveness for you unless I am forbidden." Then verses 9:113 and 28:56 were revealed forbidding this.
