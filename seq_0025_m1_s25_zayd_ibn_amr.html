Zayd ibn 'Amr ibn Nufayl

The Solitary Believer
أمة وحده
A Nation By Himself

Theological Purity

He publicly rejected the idols of Quraysh and refused to eat meat slaughtered in their names, challenging the logic of his people.

"Allah created the sheep and sent down rain for it... yet you slaughter it in the name of other than Him?"

Social Justice

He actively fought against the practice of female infanticide (Wa'd al-Banāt). He would take the daughters from their fathers and raise them himself.

"Do not kill her. I will suffice you her provision."
"He will be resurrected on the Day of Judgment as a nation (Ummah) by himself."
Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
