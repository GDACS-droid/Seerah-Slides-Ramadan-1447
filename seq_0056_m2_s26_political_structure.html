Makkah's Unique Political Structure

An Oligarchy of Free Tribes

Monarchy (Mulk)

The standard model of the time (Rome, Persia, Yemen). A single King rules by decree and force.

"We will not bow to any man."
— The Arab Ethos

VS

Tribal Oligarchy

A decentralized confederation of equal clans. Power relied on prestige, wealth, and lineage, not executive force.

الملأ
Al-Malā' (The Council)
The assembly of chiefs at Dār al-Nadwah. Decisions required consensus.
"A city-state without a police force, held together by tradition and mutual interest."
