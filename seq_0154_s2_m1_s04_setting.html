The Setting: Ghār Ḥirā'

Geography of the First Revelation
Location of Ghār Ḥirā' on Jabal al-Nūr, northeast of Makkah
غار حراء

Elevation

The cave sits near the summit of Jabal al-Nūr (Mountain of Light) at approximately 634 metres above sea level. The climb from the base takes roughly 1.5–2 hours on foot.

Distance from the Ka'bah

Approximately 4 km (2.5 miles) northeast of the Ka'bah in a direct line. The cave opening faces the Ka'bah.

The Cave Itself

A small opening roughly 3.7 m long and 1.6 m wide, just large enough for one person to sit and reflect in seclusion (Taḥannuth).

The Moment

The Prophet ﷺ at age 40, in the month of Ramaḍān, alone with provisions prepared by Khadījah (ra). A heart spiritually prepared but unaware of what was coming.
