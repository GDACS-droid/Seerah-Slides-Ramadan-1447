The First Commands

Sūrah al-Muddaththir (74:1-5)
1
قُمْ فَأَنذِرْ
Arise and warn
Transition from private contemplation to public mission.
2
وَرَبَّكَ فَكَبِّرْ
And your Lord glorify
Establish the greatness of Allah above all idols.
3
وَثِيَابَكَ فَطَهِّرْ
And your clothing purify
Internal and external purity is a prerequisite for dawah.
4
وَالرُّجْزَ فَاهْجُرْ
And uncleanliness avoid
Complete dissociation from idol worship.
This marked the end of his private life and the beginning of his public struggle.
