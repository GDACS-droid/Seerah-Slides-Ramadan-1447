Significance of Ḥarb al-Fijār

What Did He Witness?
Destructiveness
He saw firsthand the futility of tribal warfare, where lives were lost for petty reasons and pride.
Violation of Sanctity
He witnessed the breaking of sacred norms (Ḥurmah) during the holy months, showing the moral decay of Arabia.
Cycle of Revenge
He observed the endless loop of Tha'r (blood revenge) that perpetuated violence across generations.
Future Impact
"This experience likely shaped his later emphasis on reconciliation, peace treaties, and the sanctity of life, contrasting sharply with the Jahiliyyah mindset."
