Key Takeaways

Module 4: Al-Yatīm al-Karīm
Orphanhood as Selection
His orphanhood was not a random tragedy but a divine design to detach him from worldly reliance and attach him solely to Allah.
The Power of Barakah
His presence in the desert brought tangible abundance to Ḥalīmah's household, proving his blessed nature long before Prophethood.
Spiritual Preparation
The incident of Shaqq al-Ṣadr (Opening of the Chest) marked his physical and spiritual purification, setting him apart from all others.
Divine Guardianship
Allah replaced every lost guardian with another, ensuring he was protected by the best of Makkah ('Abd al-Muṭṭalib, then Abū Ṭālib).
"He found you an orphan and gave you shelter."
