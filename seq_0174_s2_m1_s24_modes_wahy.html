Modes of Revelation

The Methodology of Waḥy
True Dreams الرؤيا الصادقة
The beginning of revelation; dreams that came true like daylight.
Inspiration النفث في الروع
Knowledge cast directly into the heart/mind without seeing an angel.
Ringing of a Bell صلصلة الجرس
The most difficult form; intense weight and sweating.
Angel in Human Form تمثل الملك رجلاً
Easiest form; often appeared as Dihyah al-Kalbi.
Angel in True Form صورته الحقيقية
Occurred only twice (Cave Hira & Sidrat al-Muntaha).
Direct Speech كلام الله
Behind a veil; occurred during Isra' and Mi'raj.
