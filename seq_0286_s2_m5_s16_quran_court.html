The Qur'an in Court

Surah Maryam (Chapter 19)
"Kāf-Hā-Yā-'Ayn-Ṣād. [This is] a mention of the mercy of your Lord to His servant Zechariah..."
Ja'far recited the opening verses of Surah Maryam, which tells the story of John the Baptist (Yaḥyā) and Jesus ('Īsā) in a language that resonated deeply with the Christian court.
The Emotional Impact
The Negus wept until his beard was wet, and his bishops wept until their scrolls were soaked. The Negus declared: "Verily, this and what Jesus brought come from the same niche (Mishkāh)."
