Qur'ānic Framework: Sūrah aḍ-Ḍuḥā

Divine Validation of the Orphan's Journey
أَلَمْ يَجِدْكَ يَتِيمًا فَآوَىٰ
"Did He not find you an orphan and give [you] shelter?"
Sūrah aḍ-Ḍuḥā (93:6)
The Finding (Yatīman)
God recognizes his orphanhood; the phrase "found you" implies that Allah was always watching and present in that state.
The Sheltering (Fa Āwā)
Shelter is attributed ultimately to Allah: while others provided care, Allah was the true Provider of security.
The Ethical Command (93:9)
"So as for the orphan, do not oppress [him]." — A concise ethical injunction to protect the vulnerable.
الإِيوَاء Al-Īwā' (The Act of Sheltering)
