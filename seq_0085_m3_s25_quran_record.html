Sūrah al-Fīl: The Qur'ānic Record

Chapter 105
أَلَمْ تَرَ كَيْفَ فَعَلَ رَبُّكَ بِأَصْحَابِ الْفِيلِ
أَلَمْ يَجْعَلْ كَيْدَهُمْ فِي تَضْلِيلٍ
وَأَرْسَلَ عَلَيْهِمْ طَيْرًا أَبَابِيلَ
"Have you not considered how your Lord dealt with the Companions of the Elephant? Did He not make their plan into misguidance? And He sent against them birds in flocks..."
أَبَابِيل
Abābīl

"In successive flocks" — not a single species, but swarms.

سِجِّيل
Sijjīl

Baked clay stones; a decreed punishment.

عَصْفٍ مَأْكُول
'Aṣf Ma'kūl

"Eaten straw" — imagery of total disintegration.

"The event was so well-known that the Qur'ān appeals to their collective memory ('Did you not see?')."
