The Paradigm Case

The Offer

"I am the wealthiest of the Anṣār. Take half of my wealth. I have two wives; look at which one pleases you more, and I will divorce her for you to marry."

Sa'd ibn al-Rabī'
Demonstrates the Anṣār's total commitment and selflessness (Īthār).

The Response

"May Allāh bless you in your family and wealth. I have no need for this. Just show me the way to the marketplace."

'Abd al-Raḥmān ibn 'Awf
Demonstrates the Muhājirūn's dignity ('Iffah) and desire for self-sufficiency.
