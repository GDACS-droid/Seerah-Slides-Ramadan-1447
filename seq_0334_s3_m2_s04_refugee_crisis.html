The Problem: Refugee Crisis

The Muhājirūn arrived in Madīnah destitute and displaced.

Total Dispossession The Meccan emigrants left behind homes, businesses, and wealth. Suhayb al-Rūmī gave up his entire fortune for safe passage.
Skill Mismatch The Muhājirūn were traders and merchants in an agrarian economy. They lacked farming skills and land ownership.
Psychological & Physical Toll Many suffered from homesickness and the "Fever of Madīnah" (Ḥummā al-Madīnah). 'Ā'ishah described the initial period as one of severe sickness.
100% Asset Loss
0 Social Safety Net
