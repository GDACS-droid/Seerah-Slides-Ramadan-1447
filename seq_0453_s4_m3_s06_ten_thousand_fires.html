10,000 Fires

Psychological Warfare at Marr al-Ẓahrān

The Horizon Ablaze
The Tactic: The Prophet ﷺ ordered every single soldier to light a separate fire. Usually, 10 soldiers share one fire. This created the illusion of an army far larger than 10,000, striking terror into the scouts of Quraysh.

The Submission of Abū Sufyān

Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ

Abū Sufyān
