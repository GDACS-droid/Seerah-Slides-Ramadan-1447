The Heart of the State

وظائف المسجد النبوي

The Prophet's Mosque was not merely a place of prayer; it was the center of gravity for the entire Ummah, serving multiple vital functions simultaneously.

Spiritual Center

The primary space for the five daily prayers, Jumu'ah, and spiritual retreat (I'tikāf).

Political Headquarters

The site for receiving foreign delegations, conducting Shūrā (consultation), and issuing decrees.

Educational Institute

Home to the Ahl al-Ṣuffah and the primary circle for teaching the Qur'an and Sunnah.

Social Welfare & Military

A shelter for the homeless, a hospital for the wounded during battles, and a command center.
