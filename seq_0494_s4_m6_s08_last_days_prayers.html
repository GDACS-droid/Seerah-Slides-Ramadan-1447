The Last Days

Transition of Leadership

The Command

When the Prophet ﷺ became too weak to go to the mosque, he gave a clear order:

"Order Abū Bakr to lead the people in prayer."

He repeated this three times, establishing Abū Bakr as the spiritual leader of the community.

The Last Prayer Led

One day, feeling a slight relief, he went out supported by two men. Abū Bakr stepped back, but the Prophet ﷺ signaled him to stay.

The Prophet ﷺ sat to the left of Abū Bakr and led the prayer sitting, while Abū Bakr followed him standing, and the people followed Abū Bakr.

The Final Look (Monday Fajr)

He lifted the curtain of Aisha's room and looked at the Muslims praying in rows. He smiled, his face "like a page of the Quran."
It was a smile of contentment—witnessing the fruit of his 23-year mission: a united Ummah worshipping Allah.
