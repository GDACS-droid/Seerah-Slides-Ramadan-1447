The Pursuit of Surāqah

The Hunter Becomes the Guardian
Wanted: Dead or Alive — Reward: 100 Camels
The Chase
Surāqah b. Mālik, a skilled tracker, spotted them. Driven by greed, he galloped towards them, spear in hand.
The Miracle
As he approached, his horse's legs sank into the solid earth up to its knees. He tried three times, failing each time, until he realized this was divine protection.
The Reversal
He begged for safety. The Prophet ﷺ granted it. Surāqah then turned back other pursuers, saying "I have checked this path for you."
"O Surāqah, how will you feel when you wear the bracelets of Kisrā (Chosroes)?"
(Fulfilled years later during the Caliphate of 'Umar b. Al-Khaṭṭāb when the Persian Empire fell.)
