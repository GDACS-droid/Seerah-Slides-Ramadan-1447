The Break of Dawn

Sūrah al-Ḍuḥā
مَا وَدَّعَكَ رَبُّكَ وَمَا قَلَى
"Your Lord has not taken leave of you, [O Muhammad], nor has He detested [you]."

The silence was broken not with a command, but with reassurance. This Sūrah was revealed to heal his heart, confirming that the pause was part of the divine plan, not a punishment.
