The Custodians Before Quraysh

The Historical Shift of Power in Makkah
Era I

Banū Jurhum

  • The in-laws of Ismā'īl (peace be upon him).
  • Ruled Makkah for centuries but eventually became corrupt and oppressive.
  • Key Event: Before being expelled, they buried the Zamzam well to hide it.
Expelled due to corruption
Era II

Banū Khuzā'ah

  • A Qaḥṭānī tribe that seized control from Jurhum.
  • Politically dominant but religiously innovative.
  • Key Figure: 'Amr ibn Luḥay, who introduced idolatry to the Ka'bah.

The Context

Quraysh

  • Descendants of Fihr living in the outskirts.
  • Scattered sub-clans with no central leadership.
  • Status: "Quraysh al-Ẓawāhir" (People of the Outskirts) — powerless and disenfranchised.
