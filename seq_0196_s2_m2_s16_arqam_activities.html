Activities in Dār al-Arqam

Building the Core
Education (Ta'līm)
Memorizing new verses, understanding their meanings, and internalizing the new worldview.
Purification (Tazkiyah)
Cleansing the heart of Jahiliyyah traits: arrogance, tribalism, and idol worship.
Brotherhood (Ukhuwwah)
Forging bonds stronger than blood. A master sat next to a slave, a noble next to a commoner.
It was an intensive training camp that produced the "Golden Generation" of Companions.
