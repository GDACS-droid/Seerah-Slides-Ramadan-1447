The Peaceful March

The Pilgrims

1,400

Companions marched out, including the Prophet's wife Umm Salamah. Their sheer number showed strength, but their attire showed peace.

The State of Iḥrām

They wore the two white sheets of Iḥrām. No armor. No shields. No spears. They carried only "Traveler's Swords" which remained sheathed in their scabbards.

The Symbol

70

Camels (Hady) were garlanded (Qalā'id). In Arab custom, this was an inviolable sign that these people were guests of God, not invaders.

Strategic Masterstroke: By entering in this state, the Prophet ﷺ trapped Quraysh. If they attacked pilgrims, they would violate the sanctity of the Haram and lose their legitimacy as Custodians of the House.
