The Death of Khadījah (ra)

The Loss of Internal Comfort
"She believed in me when people rejected me, she shared her wealth with me when people denied me, and Allah blessed me with children through her when He denied me children through others."
— Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ (Musnad Aḥmad)

Emotional Sanctuary

Khadījah was the Prophet's ﷺ refuge. When the world outside was hostile, his home was a place of peace, validation, and unconditional love.

Unwavering Support

She exhausted her vast wealth to support the Muslims during the boycott, sacrificing her comfort for the sake of the message.

Eternal Love

Even years after her death, the Prophet ﷺ would honor her memory, sending gifts to her friends and speaking of her with deep affection.
