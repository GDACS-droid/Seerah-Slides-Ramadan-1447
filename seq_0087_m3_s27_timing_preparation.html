Global Attention

The miraculous destruction of Abrahah's army turned the eyes of the world toward Makkah. The stage was lit as the Prophet ﷺ was about to arrive.

Divine Sanctity

God showed He is the direct Protector of this House. This established the sanctity of Makkah to prepare for the sanctity of the person (Muhammad ﷺ).
