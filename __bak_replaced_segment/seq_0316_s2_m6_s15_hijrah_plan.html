The Plot and the Plan

Dār al-Nadwah vs. Divine Decree

The Conspiracy

Location: Dār al-Nadwah • Attendee: "Sheikh Najdī" (Iblīs)
  • Option 1: Imprison him in irons until he dies.
    Rejected: His followers would free him.
  • Option 2: Exile him from the land.
    Rejected: His eloquence would win over other tribes.
  • Option 3: Assassination. One young man from every tribe strikes simultaneously. Banū Hāshim cannot fight all Arabs and will accept blood money.

The Divine Counter-Plan

Source: Revelation (Waḥy) via Jibrīl (as)
  • The Command: Jibrīl (as) informed the Prophet ﷺ of the plot and commanded him not to sleep in his bed that night.
  • The Strategy:
    1. Leave during the heat of noon (unusual time).
    2. Go to the house of Abū Bakr.
    3. Prepare two camels.
    4. Hire a non-Muslim guide (Abdullah b. Urayqiṭ).
"And [remember, O Muhammad], when those who disbelieved plotted against you to restrain you or kill you or evict you. But they plan, and Allah plans. And Allah is the best of planners." (Surah Al-Anfal 8:30)
