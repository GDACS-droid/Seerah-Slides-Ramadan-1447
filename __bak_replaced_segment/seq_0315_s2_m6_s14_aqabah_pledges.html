The Pledges of 'Aqabah

The Seeds of the State

The First Pledge

12th Year of Prophethood
Attendees 12 Men
Nature Ethical & Religious
  • To worship Allah alone (Tawḥīd).
  • To refrain from theft, adultery, and killing children.
  • To obey the Prophet ﷺ in all that is good.
Outcome
Muṣ'ab b. 'Umayr sent to Yathrib as the first ambassador/teacher.

The Second Pledge

13th Year of Prophethood
Attendees 73 Men, 2 Women
Nature Political & Defensive
  • To listen and obey in ease and hardship.
  • To protect the Prophet ﷺ as they protect their own families.
  • "Blood is blood, and destruction is destruction."
Outcome
The migration (Hijrah) to Yathrib became mandated.
