The Logic of Escalation

Why Persecution Began
Failure of Soft Power
When mockery, propaganda, and negotiations failed to stop the spread of Islam, Quraysh felt compelled to use physical force.
Phase 1: Words
Ridicule, poetry, and character assassination.
Phase 2: Deals
Offers of wealth, power, and kingship.
Phase 3: Force
Torture, imprisonment, and physical abuse.
