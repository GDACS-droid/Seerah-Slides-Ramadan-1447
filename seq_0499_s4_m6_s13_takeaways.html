Key Takeaways

Summary of Module 6

The Universal Charter

The Farewell Sermon established the foundational principles of human rights, economic justice, and social equality in Islam.

The Perfection of Religion

The revelation of Verse 5:3 marked the completion of the legislative mission, leaving no room for innovation in the core of the Deen.

The Human Messenger

The Prophet's illness and passing confirmed his humanity. He was the perfect servant and messenger, but worship belongs to Allah alone.

The Continuity of Leadership

The immediate transition to Abu Bakr's Caliphate preserved the political unity of the Ummah and ensured the message would continue beyond the Messenger.

"Indeed, to Allah we belong and to Him we shall return."
