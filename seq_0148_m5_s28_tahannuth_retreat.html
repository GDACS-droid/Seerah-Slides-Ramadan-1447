Retreat to Ghār Ḥirā': Taḥannuth

The Spiritual Preparation
Definition
Linguistically derived from Ḥinth (sin), Taḥannuth means to shun sin or to engage in worship to purify oneself from it. It was a practice of the Ḥunafā'.

  • The Timing

    He would retreat for multiple nights, often spending the entire month of Ramadan in seclusion.

  • The Provision

    He would return to Khadijah only to replenish his supplies (Zād), highlighting her crucial support role.

Jabal al-Nūr

The Mountain of Light
The Cave: A small, narrow opening at the summit, providing isolation from the noise of Makkah.
The View: The cave faces the Ka'bah directly, allowing him to combine seclusion with visual connection to the House of Allah.
The Atmosphere: Absolute silence, vast sky, and distance from the idolatry of the city below.
