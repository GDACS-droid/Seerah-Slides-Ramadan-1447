The Trial of Ka'b ibn Mālik

Truthfulness vs. Excuses

1

The Confession

While over 80 hypocrites made up false excuses to avoid punishment, Ka'b chose the bitter truth: "I have no excuse. I was stronger and wealthier than ever before."

2

The Boycott

The Prophet ﷺ ordered a total social boycott. For 50 days, no one spoke to him. The earth, vast as it is, felt narrow and suffocating.

3

The Redemption

Revelation descended (9:118) accepting his repentance. His truthfulness saved him, while the lies of the hypocrites destroyed them.

"O Ka'b, rejoice in the best day that has passed over you since your mother gave birth to you."
