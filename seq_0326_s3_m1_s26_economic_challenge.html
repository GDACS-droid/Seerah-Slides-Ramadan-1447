The Economic Challenge

بناء السوق الإسلامي

The Status Quo

  • Market dominated by Banū Qaynuqā'
  • System based on Ribā (Usury)
  • High taxes and fees
  • Economic dependency

The Prophetic Solution

  • Established a new independent market
  • Abolished taxes (Free Trade Zone)
  • Prohibited Ribā and fraud
  • Ensured fair competition

"This is your market; let no tax be levied on it."

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ upon establishing the Souq
