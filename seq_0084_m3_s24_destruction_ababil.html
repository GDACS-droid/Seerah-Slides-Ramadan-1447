The Destruction: Birds of Abābīl

The Divine Defense of the Sanctuary
طَيْرًا أَبَابِيل
Birds in Flocks

Swarms of birds appeared from the direction of the sea. "Abābīl" refers to the formation (swarms), not a specific species.

حِجَارَةً مِّن سِجِّيل
Stones of Baked Clay

Each bird carried three stones (one in beak, two in claws). Though small, they were divinely charged to pierce through armor and flesh.

عَصْفٍ مَّأْكُول
Eaten Straw

The army was decimated, their bodies left like hollowed-out stalks or chewed straw, utterly destroyed and scattered.

"The protection of the Ka'bah was entirely miraculous, with no human intervention."
