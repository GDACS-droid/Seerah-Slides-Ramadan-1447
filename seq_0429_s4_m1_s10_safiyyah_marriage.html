Marriage to Ṣafiyyah bint Ḥuyayy

Restoration of Dignity & Status

Noble Lineage

She was the daughter of Ḥuyayy ibn Akhṭab, the chief of Banū Naḍīr. A direct descendant of Prophet Hārūn (Aaron) عليه السلام.

Captured after the fall of Khaybar, the Prophet ﷺ recognized that her status as the "daughter of a king" made her unsuitable for anyone but the leader of the Muslims, to preserve her honor.

The Choice & Mahr

The Prophet ﷺ offered her a choice:
1. Freedom and return to her people.
2. Freedom and marriage to him.

She chose Allāh and His Messenger. He emancipated her, and her freedom was her dowry (Mahr), setting a legal precedent.

"My father is Aaron, my uncle is Moses, and my husband is Muhammad."

The Prophet ﷺ taught her these words to defend her honor against jealousy
