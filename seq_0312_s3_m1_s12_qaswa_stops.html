The Camel Kneels

بروك الناقة
"Let her be, for she is commanded."
دَعُوهَا فَإِنَّهَا مَأْمُورَةٌ

The Divine Selection

Every clan in Madīnah tried to take the reins of Al-Qaṣwā', hoping for the honor of hosting the Prophet ﷺ. By refusing them gently and attributing the choice to divine command, the Prophet ﷺ avoided showing favoritism to any tribe, preventing internal jealousy from day one.

The Final Stop

She finally knelt at a Mirbad (drying floor for dates) belonging to two orphans, Sahl and Suhayl, from the clan of Banū al-Najjār (the Prophet's maternal relatives).
