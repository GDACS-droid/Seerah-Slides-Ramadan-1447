The Death of Sons: A Theological Note

Divine Wisdom in Tragedy
"Muḥammad is not the father of [any] one of your men, but [he is] the Messenger of Allah and last of the prophets."
(Quran 33:40)
Preventing Dynastic Succession
Had a son survived, the Arabs might have viewed Prophethood as a hereditary kingship, expecting the son to inherit the mantle of leadership or revelation.
Ensuring Finality
The death of his male offspring in infancy closed the door to any confusion regarding the "Seal of Prophethood." No male heir existed to challenge the finality of his message.
خَاتَمُ النَّبِيِّين Khātam al-Nabiyyīn (Seal of the Prophets)
