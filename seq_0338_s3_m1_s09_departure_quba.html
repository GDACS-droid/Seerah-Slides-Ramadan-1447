Departure Toward Madīnah

الرحيل إلى المدينة

After approximately four days in Qubā', the Prophet ﷺ mounted his camel, Al-Qaṣwā', on Friday morning and departed toward the heart of Yathrib. Along the way, he stopped at Wādī Rānūnā' to lead the first Jumu'ah prayer.

Every Anṣārī clan along the route hoped to host the Prophet ﷺ.
"The camel is commanded — she will kneel where Allah wills."
