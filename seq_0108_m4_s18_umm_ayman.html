Status
The Prophet's Inheritance
She was the only property left by his father, 'Abdullāh.
The Constant Witness
She is unique in Seerah history: she cradled him at birth, cared for him in childhood, witnessed his marriage, believed in his Prophethood, and outlived him.
The Journey Home
After Āminah died at Al-Abwā', it was Barakah who held the 6-year-old boy, wiped his tears, and brought him safely back to Makkah.
