The Dilemma of Adab

The Prophet ﷺ chose the ground floor for the convenience of his visitors. However, Abū Ayyūb and his wife were distressed at the thought of walking on the floor above the Prophet ﷺ, considering it a breach of respect.

"How can I walk on a ceiling under which is the Messenger of Allah?"

They would walk only on the edges of the room to avoid being directly above him, until the Prophet ﷺ eventually agreed to move upstairs to put their hearts at ease.
