The Sharaf System

The Hierarchy of Quraysh
The Pillars of Nobility

Nasab (Lineage)

Purity of blood and connection to Quṣayy.

Māl (Wealth)

Success in trade caravans and commercial influence.

'Izzah (Manpower)

Number of sons, brothers, and tribal allies.

Karam (Generosity)

Feeding pilgrims and slaughtering camels for guests.
The Social Divide
Quraysh al-Biṭāḥ
The Valley Dwellers

The inner circle living directly around the Ka'bah. They held political power, the key offices, and the highest prestige.

Quraysh al-Ẓawāhir
The Outskirts Dwellers

Those living in the surrounding hills. They served as the city's defenders but held less political sway in Dār al-Nadwah.

"The Prophet ﷺ came from the absolute peak of this hierarchy: Quraysh al-Biṭāḥ, Banū Hāshim."
