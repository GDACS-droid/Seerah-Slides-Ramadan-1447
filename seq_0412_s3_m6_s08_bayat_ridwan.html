Bay'at al-Riḥwān

The Trigger: 'Uthmān ibn 'Affān was sent as an envoy to Makkah but was delayed. A rumor reached the camp: "Uthmān has been killed." The mood shifted instantly from peaceful pilgrimage to readiness for war.

The Pledge Under the Tree

The Prophet ﷺ sat under a Samurah tree and called the 1,400 companions to pledge allegiance. They pledged to fight to the death (or not to flee) to avenge 'Uthmān.

The Prophet ﷺ clasped his own hands together and said: "This is for 'Uthmān."

"Certainly was Allāh pleased with the believers when they pledged allegiance to you under the tree, and He knew what was in their hearts, so He sent down tranquility upon them..."

Surah Al-Fatḥ (48:18)
