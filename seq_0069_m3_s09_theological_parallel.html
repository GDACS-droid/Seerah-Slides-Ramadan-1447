The Theological Parallel

Two Sacrifices, Two Ransoms
The First Sacrifice

Ibrāhīm was commanded to sacrifice his son Ismā'īl as a test of submission.

Ransomed by a Ram
The Second Sacrifice

'Abd al-Muṭṭalib vowed to sacrifice his son 'Abdullāh as a test of fidelity.

Ransomed by 100 Camels
The Prophet ﷺ Said:
أَنَا ابْنُ الذَّبِيحَيْن
"I am the son of the two sacrificed ones."
A lineage purified by the willingness to sacrifice for God.
