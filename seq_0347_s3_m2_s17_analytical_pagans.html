Pagans as Signatories

Why were Polytheists included in the Charter?

Given the strict monotheism of Islam, how could idolaters be part of the political community?

Distinction in Hostility

The Prophet ﷺ distinguished between the aggressive Quraysh (Ḥarbī) and the peaceful pagans of Madīnah (Mu'āhid). Shirk (polytheism) itself did not preclude political alliance.

Gradualism (Tadarruj)

Islam did not demand immediate conversion. The strategy was to secure the internal front first, allowing time for da'wah to work naturally.

One Condition

The only non-negotiable condition was: "No polytheist shall protect any property or person of Quraysh."
