The Scene of Grief
The young boy was seen walking behind his grandfather's bier, weeping openly. It was a rare display of public emotion, marking the depth of his attachment to the man who had been his sole protector.
The Shift in Status
With 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib's death, the "golden era" of being the Chief's favorite ended. He now faced a transition from the center of power to the care of a loving but financially struggling uncle.
