Umar's Denial

Overwhelmed by grief, Umar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb drew his sword and declared: "Some hypocrites say the Messenger of Allah has died... By Allah, he has only gone to his Lord as Moses went... and he will return!"

Madīnah was plunged into darkness and confusion. The people were weeping, unable to comprehend the loss.
"Whoever worshipped Muhammad, then Muhammad is dead. But whoever worshipped Allah, then Allah is Ever-Living and does not die."
Abū Bakr al-Ṣiddīq
"Muhammad is not but a messenger. [Other] messengers have passed on before him. So if he was to die or be killed, would you turn back on your heels?"
Surah Ali 'Imran (3:144)
