The Passing

To the Highest Companion

The Last Act

Abd al-Rahman ibn Abi Bakr entered with a Siwak. The Prophet ﷺ looked at it longingly. Aisha softened it with her own teeth and gave it to him. It was the last worldly object to touch his lips.

The Choice

He was leaning against Aisha's chest. He raised his hand, his gaze fixed upwards. He had been given a choice between remaining in the world or meeting his Lord.

بَلِ الرَّفِيقُ الْأَعْلَى
"Nay, (to) the Highest Companion."

His hand dropped. The seal of Prophethood was complete.

Monday, 12th Rabi' al-Awwal, 11 AH • Mid-morning (Duha)
