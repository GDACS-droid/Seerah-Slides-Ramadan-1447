Distribution at Ji'rānah

Winning Hearts with Generosity

The Strategy
المُؤَلَّفَةِ قُلُوبُهُم
"Those whose hearts are to be reconciled."
Giving massive material wealth to the new converts (Quraysh chiefs) to secure their loyalty and soften their hearts towards Islam.
Abū Sufyān
100 Camels + 40 oz Silver
Ḥakīm ibn Ḥizām
100 Camels (then +100 more)
Ṣafwān ibn Umayyah
A valley full of sheep

"By Allah, he gave me what he gave me while he was the most hated of people to me, and he continued to give to me until he became the most beloved of people to me."

— Ṣafwān ibn Umayyah
