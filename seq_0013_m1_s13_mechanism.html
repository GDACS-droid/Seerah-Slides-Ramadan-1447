The Mechanism of Theological Corruption

Shirk Did Not Replace Tawḥīd Overnight — It Infiltrated Gradually
01
The Foundation Remains
Pre-Islamic Arabs never denied Allah's existence. They acknowledged Him as the Supreme Creator and Sustainer.
02
The Deviation: Intermediaries (Shufa' / شفعاء)
The corruption began by seeking "closeness" (Zulfa) to Allah through created beings.
03
The Justification
"We only worship them that they may bring us nearer to Allah in position." (Qur'an 39:3)
Theological Classification
Shirk fī al-'ibādah (شرك في العبادة)
Associating partners in acts of worship, while retaining belief in Allah's Lordship.
