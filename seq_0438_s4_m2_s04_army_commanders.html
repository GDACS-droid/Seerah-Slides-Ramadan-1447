The Army of Commanders

The Chain of Command & The Prophecy

1

Zayd ibn Ḥārithah

"The Beloved of the Messenger." The only Companion mentioned by name in the Quran. First time leading such a major force.

2

Ja'far ibn Abī Ṭālib

"The Father of the Poor." Recently returned from Abyssinia. Known for his eloquence and resemblance to the Prophet ﷺ.

3

'Abdullāh ibn Rawāḥah

The Poet of the Prophet ﷺ. A leader of the Anṣār who encouraged the army to march forward towards "one of two victories."

The Prophetic Decree

"If Zayd is killed, then Ja'far is the commander; and if Ja'far is killed, then 'Abdullāh ibn Rawāḥah is the commander."
