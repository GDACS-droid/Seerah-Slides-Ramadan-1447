The Arrival of 'Alī ibn Abī Ṭālib

وصول علي بن أبي طالب

Mission of Integrity

'Alī stayed behind in Makkah for three days to return the Amānāt (trusts/deposits) to their owners. Remarkably, these owners were the same Quraysh who were plotting to kill the Prophet ﷺ.

The Arduous Journey

He traveled alone, mostly on foot, hiding during the day and walking at night. By the time he reached Qubā', his feet were severely swollen and bleeding.

Upon seeing 'Alī's condition, the Prophet ﷺ wept out of compassion and applied his saliva to 'Alī's feet, healing them instantly.
