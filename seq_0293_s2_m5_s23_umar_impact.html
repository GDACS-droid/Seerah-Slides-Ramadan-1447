The Impact of 'Umar

Islam Goes Public
Public Prayer
Immediately after his conversion, 'Umar demanded: "Are we not on the truth?" He then led the Muslims to pray openly at the Ka'bah for the first time.
Two Pillars
With Ḥamzah and 'Umar, the Muslims now had two powerful protectors. The Dā'wah shifted from purely defensive secrecy to a bold public presence.
"We have been powerful/honored ever since 'Umar accepted Islam."
- 'Abdullāh ibn Mas'ūd
