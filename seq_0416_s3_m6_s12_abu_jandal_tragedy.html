The Test: Abu Jandal

The Arrival: Just as the treaty was concluded, Abu Jandal (the son of the negotiator Suhayl ibn 'Amr) stumbled into the camp, bound in chains, having escaped from torture in Makkah.

The Demand

"O Muhammad! This is the first one I demand you return to me under the treaty."

Suhayl struck his son and refused to proceed with the peace unless Abu Jandal was returned immediately.

The Plea

"O Muslims! Am I to be returned to the polytheists to be tested in my religion?"

His cry caused immense distress among the Companions, who wept at the sight of his condition.

"O Abu Jandal, be patient and seek reward with Allāh. Allāh will make a way out for you and those with you. We have given them our word, and we do not betray."

The Prophetic Consolation
