Riḥlat al-Shitā' wa al-Ṣayf

The Twin Lifelines of Makkah
رحلة الصيف
The Summer Journey
Destination: Al-Shām (Syria/Byzantium)
Purpose: Access to Roman markets, grains, and luxury goods during the cooler northern season.
"For the accustomed security of Quraysh — Their accustomed security in the winter and summer journeys..."
Sūrah Quraysh (106:1-2)
رحلة الشتاء
The Winter Journey
Destination: Yemen (Ḥimyar/Abyssinia)
Purpose: Access to spices, perfumes, and silks during the warmer southern season.
"Allah linked their economic prosperity directly to their worship of the Lord of the House."
