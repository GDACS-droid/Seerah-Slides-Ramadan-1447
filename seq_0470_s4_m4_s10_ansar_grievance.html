The Tears of the Anṣār

The Ultimate Share

The Whisperings

"The Messenger of Allah has found his people... He gives to Quraysh while our swords are still dripping with their blood."

The Prophet ﷺ gathered the Anṣār in a tent, excluding all others, to address their hearts directly.

"Are you not satisfied, O Anṣār, that the people go home with sheep and camels, while you go home with the Messenger of Allah?"

"If the people walked in a valley and the Anṣār walked in another, I would walk in the valley of the Anṣār."

The Result: They wept until their beards were wet, saying:
"We are satisfied with the Messenger of Allah as our share!"
