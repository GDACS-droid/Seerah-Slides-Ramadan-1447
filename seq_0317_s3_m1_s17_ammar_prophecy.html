The Prophecy of 'Ammār

نبوءة عمار بن ياسر

During the construction, while others carried one brick at a time, 'Ammār ibn Yāsir carried two. The Prophet ﷺ saw him covered in dust, wiped his head, and made a profound statement that would echo through Islamic history.

"Woe to 'Ammār! The rebellious group will kill him."
وَيْحَ عَمَّارٍ تَقْتُلُهُ الْفِئَةُ الْبَاغِيَةُ
"He invites them to Paradise, and they invite him to the Fire."
Major Proof of Prophethood & Political Marker
