Key Clauses: The Muslim Community

Clause 1: The Ummah
"They are a single community (Ummah) to the exclusion of all other people."
Established faith, not lineage, as the primary political identity.
Clause 12: Collective Responsibility
"A believer shall not leave a debtor among them heavy with debt without paying his share of blood money or ransom."
Established a social welfare system and collective insurance.
Clause 15: Universal Protection
"The protection of God is one; the least of them may give protection [to a stranger] that is binding on all."
Democratized the right to grant asylum, previously a chief's privilege.
