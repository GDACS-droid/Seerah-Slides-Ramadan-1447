The Evacuation of Makkah

The Strategic Withdrawal

"O People of Quraysh! Take shelter in the mountain passes. Do not expose yourselves to destruction against a force you cannot defeat."

The High Ground

The entire population retreated to the peaks and valleys surrounding Makkah, watching from above in safety.

The Empty City

Makkah was left completely deserted. The Ka'bah stood alone in the valley, with no human defenders to protect it.

شِعَابُ مَكَّة Shi'āb Makkah (The Mountain Passes)
"The city was left ghost-like, entrusting its defense entirely to the Divine."
