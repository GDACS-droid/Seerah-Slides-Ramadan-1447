The Shepherd: A Prophetic Occupation

"Every Prophet Herded Sheep"
مَا بَعَثَ اللَّهُ نَبِيًّا إِلاَّ رَعَى الْغَنَمَ
"Allah never sent a Prophet except that he herded sheep."
Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī
The Question
His companions asked in surprise:
"Even you, O Messenger of Allah?"
The Answer
"Yes, I used to herd them for the people of Makkah for a few Qīrāṭ (small wages)."
الرَّعْي Al-Ra'ī (Shepherding)
