Intelligence Gathering

The Scouts

The Prophet ﷺ dispatched two trusted companions ahead of the main army to scout the wells of Badr:

  • • Basbas ibn 'Amr al-Juhanī
  • • 'Adī ibn Abī al-Zaghbā' al-Juhanī

The Intel

They successfully infiltrated the area near the wells and overheard two water-carriers confirming that the caravan would arrive "tomorrow or the day after."

Strategic Insight

The Prophet ﷺ did not move blindly. He prioritized accurate, timely information over sheer numbers, establishing intelligence as a cornerstone of military strategy.
