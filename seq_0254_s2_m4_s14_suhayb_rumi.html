Ṣuhayb al-Rūmī

The Profitable Transaction
A wealthy merchant, Ṣuhayb was stopped by Quraysh when attempting to migrate. They told him he came to Makkah penniless and could not leave with his wealth.
He offered them all his property in exchange for his freedom to migrate. Upon hearing this, the Prophet ﷺ exclaimed:
"The transaction has profited, O Abū Yaḥyā! The transaction has profited."
- Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
