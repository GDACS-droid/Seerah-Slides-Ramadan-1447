The Baḥīrā Incident: The Recognition

The Seal and the Warning
The Seal of Prophethood
Baḥīrā discovered a physical mark (Khātam) between the boy's shoulders, described in narrations as resembling a "pigeon's egg" or a raised piece of flesh—a sign found in previous scriptures.
"Take your nephew back to his country and guard him carefully against the Jews, for by Allah, if they see him and know what I know, they will surely harm him."
— Baḥīrā's Advice to Abū Ṭālib
Academic Note: While the core event is widely transmitted, the specific details (like the exact wording of the warning or the cloud) vary across narrations. Ibn Kathīr and others note weaknesses in some chains (isnād), but the story remains a staple of Seerah literature.
