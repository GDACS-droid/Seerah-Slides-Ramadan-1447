What Was He Seeking?

The Psychology of Pre-Revelation
Spiritual Alienation
Aversion to Idols: He never bowed to an idol and felt a deep, innate repulsion towards the pagan rites of Quraysh.
Dietary Purity: He refused to eat meat sacrificed on stone altars (Nuṣub) dedicated to false gods.
Moral Solitude: He withdrew from the frivolous gatherings and moral decay of Meccan society, seeking something "other."
The True Dreams
"He would not see a dream except that it came true like the breaking of day."
For six months prior to the first revelation, these vivid, truthful dreams (Al-Ru'yā al-Ṣādiqah) prepared his heart to receive the heavy reality of the Unseen.
فَلَقُ الصُّبْح Falaq al-Ṣubḥ (The Breaking of Day)
