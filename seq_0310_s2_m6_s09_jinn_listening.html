The Jinn Listen to the Quran

The Valley of Nakhlah
"Say, [O Muhammad], 'It has been revealed to me that a group of the jinn listened and said, 'Indeed, we have heard an amazing Qur'an. It guides to the right course, and we have believed in it. And we will never associate with our Lord anyone.''"
Surah Al-Jinn (72:1-2)

The Event

On his return from Ṭā'if, dejected and sorrowful, the Prophet ﷺ stopped in the valley of Nakhlah to pray Qiyām al-Layl. A group of Jinn from Naṣībīn passed by, were captivated by his recitation, and silenced each other to listen.

The Significance

  • Universal Mission: This event confirmed that his Prophethood was not just for humans (Ins) but also for the Jinn.
  • Divine Consolation: While the humans of Ṭā'if rejected him with stones, the Jinn accepted him with reverence.
  • Unseen Support: It was a reminder that Allah's plan is vast, and support can come from the Unseen when earthly doors are closed.
