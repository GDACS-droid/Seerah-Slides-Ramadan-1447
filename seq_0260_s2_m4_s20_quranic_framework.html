The Quranic Framework

Making Sense of Suffering
أَحَسِبَ النَّاسُ أَن يُتْرَكُوا أَن يَقُولُوا آمَنَّا وَهُمْ لَا يُفْتَنُونَ
"Do the people think that they will be left to say, 'We believe' and they will not be tried?" (Surah Al-Ankabut 29:2)
The Test of Truth
Suffering is not a sign of God's abandonment, but a necessary purification to distinguish true believers from hypocrites.
Historical Precedent
The Quran recounts stories of past prophets (Ashāb al-Ukhdūd, Pharaoh's magicians) to show that persecution is the sunnah (way) of the path.
