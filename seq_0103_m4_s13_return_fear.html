The Return: Ḥalīmah's Fear

The Panic in the Desert
The Alarm: Children ran to Ḥalīmah screaming, "Muḥammad has been killed!"
The Discovery: She found him standing, pale but physically unharmed.
The Fear: Terrified of spiritual affliction or possession, she decided to return him immediately.
The Calm in Makkah
The Journey: She rushed him back to Makkah, ending his stay with Banū Sa'd.
The Confrontation: Āminah asked, "Why have you brought him back when you were so eager to keep him?"
The Confession: Ḥalīmah admitted her fear of the "unseen" touching him.

"Did you fear Satan would touch him? No, by God... I knew from his conception that he was destined for something great."

— Āminah bint Wahb
