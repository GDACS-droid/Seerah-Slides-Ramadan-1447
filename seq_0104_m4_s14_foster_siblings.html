Islamic Law: Foster-siblings share the same marriage prohibitions as blood siblings.
The Reunion at Ḥunayn

Decades later, after the Battle of Ḥunayn, Al-Shaimā' was among the captives. She claimed to be the Prophet's sister. When brought to him, she showed him a bite mark he had left on her back as a child.

He recognized her, wept, and spread his own cloak for her to sit on—honoring the bond of milk.
