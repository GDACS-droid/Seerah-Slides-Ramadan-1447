Zayd's Choice: The Ultimate Character Testimony

Blood vs. Love
Zayd's father, Ḥārithah, and uncle arrived in Makkah with a large ransom to free him. They pleaded with the Prophet ﷺ to release their son.
The Prophet's Offer
"Call him and give him the choice. If he chooses you, he is yours without ransom. But if he chooses me, I will not turn away one who chooses me."
Zayd's Verdict
"I will not choose anyone over you. You are to me like a father and a mother. I have seen from this man something that makes me never want to leave him."
The Result: His father was shocked: "Do you choose slavery over freedom and your own father?" Zayd affirmed his choice. The Prophet ﷺ immediately took him to the Ka'bah and declared his adoption.
