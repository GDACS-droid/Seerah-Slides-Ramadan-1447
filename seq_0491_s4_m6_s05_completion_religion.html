The Completion of Religion

The Culmination of the Mission

الْيَوْمَ أَكْمَلْتُ لَكُمْ دِينَكُمْ وَأَتْمَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ نِعْمَتِي وَرَضِيتُ لَكُمُ الْإِسْلَامَ دِينًا
"This day I have perfected for you your religion and completed My favor upon you and have approved for you Islam as religion."
Surah Al-Ma'idah (5:3)
The Timing

Revealed on Friday, the Day of Arafah, after Asr. It was the spiritual climax of the Farewell Hajj.

The Insight

While others rejoiced, Umar ibn al-Khaṭṭāb wept. He understood the implication: "Nothing becomes perfect except that it begins to decrease." He realized the Prophet's departure was near.
