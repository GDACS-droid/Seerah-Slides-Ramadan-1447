The Impact of Ḥamzah

A Shift in Power Dynamics
Quraysh's Restraint
Quraysh realized the Prophet ﷺ was no longer defenseless. Ḥamzah was a formidable warrior whom no one dared to challenge lightly.
Muslim Morale
The Muslims felt a surge of strength and protection ("We felt honor/strength when Ḥamzah accepted Islam").
His conversion forced Quraysh to rethink their strategy of open physical abuse, as retaliation was now a real threat.
