Analytical: The Tide Turns

"Now we will invade them, and they will not invade us; we will march to them."

Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ (Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī)

The Failed Peak

The Confederates represented the maximum military effort Arabia could muster (10,000 men). Their failure signaled that the polytheists had exhausted their capacity to destroy Madīnah. They would never unite on this scale again.

Strategic Shift

The era of Survival & Defense (Badr, Uḥud, Trench) ended. The era of Initiative & Expansion (Ḥudaybiyyah, Khaybar, Makkah) began. The Muslims now dictated the terms of engagement.

Internal Front

With the elimination of Banū Qurayẓah, the "Fifth Column" within Madīnah was removed. The internal front was finally secure, allowing the Prophet ﷺ to focus entirely on external threats.
