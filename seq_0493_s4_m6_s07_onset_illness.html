The Final Illness

Late Safar, 11 AH

The Visit to Baqi'

Late at night, the Prophet ﷺ went to the graveyard of Baqi' al-Gharqad to pray for the deceased, sensing his own time was near.

The Onset of Pain

He returned with a severe headache and fever. The pain was so intense that he tied a band around his head.

Aisha (RA) "O my head!"
Prophet Muhammad ﷺ "Nay, O Aisha, O my head!"
The Request

As the illness intensified, he would ask, "Where am I tomorrow?" His wives understood his desire to be nursed by Aisha. They lovingly granted him permission.

He was moved to her room, supported by Ali and Al-Fadl ibn Abbas, his feet dragging from weakness.

"The fever upon me is equal to that of two men among you." — Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
