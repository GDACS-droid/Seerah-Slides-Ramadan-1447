The Final Chapter

1

The Completion

The Farewell Hajj, the Sermon at Arafat, and the perfection of the religion.

2

The Final Illness

The onset of pain, the last prayers led in the mosque, and the final advice to the Ummah.

3

The Departure

The passing to the Highest Companion, the shock in Madīnah, and the succession of Abū Bakr.
