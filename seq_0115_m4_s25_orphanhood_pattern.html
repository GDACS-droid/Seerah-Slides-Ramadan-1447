The Pattern of Orphanhood

A Divine Curriculum of Loss
The Father
Loss of Origin
Removed before birth — his existence owed to none, shaping an identity beyond lineage.
The Mother
Loss of Shelter
Removed at six — he learned compassion ultimately belongs to the Creator.
The Grandfather
Loss of Support
Removed at eight — social protection gone, reliance turned to the Guardian of all.
The Divine Wisdom
"Allah cut off all created means (Asbāb) from him one by one, so his heart would attach solely to the Creator."
