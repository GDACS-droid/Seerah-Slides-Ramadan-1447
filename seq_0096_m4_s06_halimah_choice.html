Ḥalīmah al-Sa'diyyah

The Reluctant Choice that Became a Destiny
01
The Economic Motive

Wet-nurses from Banū Sa'd came to Makkah specifically seeking infants from wealthy fathers to ensure generous payment.

02
The Rejection

Every nurse rejected the infant Muḥammad ﷺ. "An orphan? Who will pay us?" His lack of a father was seen as a financial risk.

03
The Last Option

Ḥalīmah arrived late and found no one else. She said to her husband: "I dislike returning empty-handed. I will take that orphan."
