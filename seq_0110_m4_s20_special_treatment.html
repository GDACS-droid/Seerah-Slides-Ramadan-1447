The Special Treatment: A Sign

The Cushion (Firāsh)
A special cushion was laid out for 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib in the shade of the Ka'bah. No one else, not even his sons, dared to sit on it out of respect.
The young Muḥammad ﷺ would come and sit directly on it. When his uncles tried to pull him back, 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib would intervene, patting his back and smiling.
دَعُوا ابْنِي ، فَوَاللهِ إِنَّ لَهُ لَشَأْنًا
"Leave my son alone, for by Allah, he has a great status."
— 'Abd al-Muṭṭalib
شَأْن Sha'n (Great Status / Matter)
